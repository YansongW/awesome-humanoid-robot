Appendix D: Major Suppliers and Enterprise Directory Wiki

This appendix has been upgraded from a "flat supplier table" to an Enterprise/Product Wiki. Each enterprise has its own dedicated page, organized by "Group/Company → Product Line → Product → Supply Chain Position", accompanied by knowledge graph entity and relationship files.

Appendix D Navigation

  • D.1 Index by Supply Chain Link
  • D.2 WAIC 2026 Exhibitor Index
  • D.3 Enterprise Wiki Homepage Directory
  • D.4 Key Product Wiki Directory

Instructions

  • Each enterprise page includes: Company Information Card, Product Lines, Representative Products (with images and specifications), Supply Chain Position, Knowledge Graph Nodes and Relationships, References.
  • Enterprises are tagged by supply chain link; a single company may belong to multiple links.
  • Product parameters are based on official public information; missing items are marked as "Not disclosed" or "-".
  • Images prioritize public URLs from the company's official website or official press releases; if unavailable, placeholders are used with source attribution.

Data Sources

  • Official WAIC 2026 and mainstream tech media reports
  • Official websites, annual reports, product manuals, and press releases of each company
  • Public market research reports and industry databases
  • Supply chain analysis in Chapter 7 of this book

Knowledge Graph Integration

Each company, product, and production technology node generated in this appendix is saved in the docs/kg/ directory in Markdown frontmatter format, and its id is consistent with the "Knowledge Graph Anchors" in the main chapters. Node and relationship files follow the Schema defined in Chapter 2.

This Wiki is continuously updated. To add an enterprise or correct parameters, please refer to the "References" section at the bottom of the page and submit the source.
