Product Overview

The Huazhong HNC-818D series is a full-featured CNC system designed for high-end CNC machine tools and complex part machining, supporting up to 9-axis control and 5-axis linkage.

The product adopts an independently controllable CNC kernel, featuring high-speed high-precision interpolation, multi-channel control, process database, and remote diagnostic capabilities. It is widely used in aerospace, automotive manufacturing, mold processing, and precision component manufacturing, representing an important domestic high-end CNC system.
