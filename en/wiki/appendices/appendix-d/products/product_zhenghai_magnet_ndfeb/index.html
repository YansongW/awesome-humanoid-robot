Product Overview

Zhenghai Magnet's high-performance sintered NdFeB magnets adopt the Nd₂Fe₁₄B-based sintered magnet technology route, covering common grades such as N, M, H, SH, UH, and EH. The company focuses on promoting low-heavy-rare-earth/heavy-rare-earth-free magnets, reducing dependence on scarce resources through grain boundary diffusion and formula optimization, while maintaining high magnetic energy product and high coercivity.

This product is widely used in new energy vehicle drive motors, wind turbine generators, energy-saving home appliances, industrial servo motors, and humanoid robot joint actuators, serving as the core material for high-torque-density motor magnetic circuits.

Selection and Deployment Recommendations

  • For cost-sensitive applications with moderate operating temperatures, low-heavy-rare-earth solutions can be prioritized for evaluation.
  • For high-temperature, high-dynamic joint motors, it is recommended to select SH/UH or higher grades and verify the demagnetization curve.
