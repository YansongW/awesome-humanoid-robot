Product Overview

Slotless coreless structure, low inertia, high dynamic response, suitable for scenarios such as robot dexterous hands and medical precision instruments. This series of products is launched by Jiangsu Leili, mainly targeting the motor/drive/precision component market, with core parameters such as 12–48 VDC, applicable to robotics, medical, and automation equipment scenarios.

As one of the representative products of Jiangsu Leili, the coreless BLDC motor is widely used in humanoid robot fingers, medical handheld instruments, drone gimbals, and other fields. The product adopts mature manufacturing processes and can provide customized interfaces, wiring methods, and control protocols according to customer requirements.
