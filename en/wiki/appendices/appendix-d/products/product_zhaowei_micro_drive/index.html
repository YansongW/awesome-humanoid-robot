Product Overview

Ultra-small volume, high reduction ratio, low noise, customizable output shaft and interface, suitable for robot end-effectors and micro joints. This series is launched by Zhaowei Machinery & Electronics, mainly targeting the micro drive / micro motor / precision gearbox market, with core parameters such as 4:1 – 1,298:1, applicable to robots, automation equipment, and precision transmission scenarios.

As one of Zhaowei's representative products, the micro drive system is widely used in mobile phones, automobiles, medical devices, and other fields. The product adopts mature manufacturing processes and can provide customized interfaces, wiring methods, and control protocols according to customer requirements.
