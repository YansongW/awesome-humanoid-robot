Product Overview

The Apex Dynamics AB Series utilizes 100% optimized helical gears and straddle-mounted high-precision bearings, offering reduction ratios of 3–10 for single stage and 12–100 for dual stage. Frame sizes range from AB042 to AB220, with rated output torque of 14–2,000 N·m. Backlash options include P0 ≤1 arcmin, P1 ≤3 arcmin, and P2 ≤5 arcmin (single stage).

The AB Series features an IP65 protection rating, an operating temperature range of -10 °C to +90 °C, and an output flange compliant with ISO 9409-1, allowing direct mounting of rotary tables or robot end-effectors. It is widely used in CNC, robotics, semiconductor, and medical applications.

Selection and Deployment Recommendations

Select the frame size based on precision grade (P0/P1/P2) and torque; for robot joints, P1 or higher is recommended, and the overturning moment should be verified.
