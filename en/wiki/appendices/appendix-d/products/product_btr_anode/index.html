Product Overview

BTR artificial graphite anode material is made from petroleum coke and needle coke as raw materials, processed through high-temperature graphitization. It features high specific capacity, high first-cycle efficiency, excellent cycling performance, and high compaction density. The product covers various application scenarios including power batteries, energy storage batteries, and consumer electronics batteries.

Mobile platforms such as humanoid robots, quadruped robots, and unmanned vehicles have high requirements for battery energy density and cycle life. As a core main material for lithium-ion batteries, BTR's artificial graphite anode directly affects battery range and lifespan.
