Product Overview

ANSI/RIA R15.06 is the core national standard for industrial robot safety in the United States, jointly published by ANSI and RIA (now A3). This standard adopts and harmonizes ISO 10218-1 and ISO 10218-2, specifying safety design, installation, operation, and maintenance requirements for industrial robots, robot systems, and integrated applications. It serves as the cornerstone for robot compliance in the North American market.

Application Scenarios

  • Safety Design of Industrial Robot Workstations: Risk assessment, safety guarding, emergency stop, and interlocking.
  • System Integration Compliance: Safety distance and layout requirements when integrating robots with peripheral equipment.
  • Collaborative Robot Deployment in North America: Force/speed limit verification combining ISO/TS 15066 and R15.06.
  • Insurance and Liability Determination: Serves as a benchmark document for accident investigation and safety audits.
