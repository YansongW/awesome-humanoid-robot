Product Overview

Built with high-precision ball bearings, low vibration, low noise, and long life, suitable for high-speed precision transmission scenarios. This series is launched by MinebeaMitsumi, primarily targeting the micro motor / bearing / sensor market, with core parameters such as 6–36 VDC, applicable to robots, drones, and precision instruments.

As one of MinebeaMitsumi's representative products, the micro BLDC motor is widely used in humanoid robot joints, drone gimbals, medical equipment, and other fields. The product adopts mature manufacturing processes and can provide customized bearings, shaft extensions, and control interfaces according to customer requirements.
