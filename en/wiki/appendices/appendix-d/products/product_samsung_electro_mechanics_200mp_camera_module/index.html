Product Overview

This module is a high-pixel camera module developed by Samsung Electro-Mechanics based on the Samsung ISOCELL 200MP image sensor, integrating high-precision optical lenses, VCM autofocus, and OIS optical image stabilization mechanisms. In addition to smartphone main cameras, its high resolution and compact design also make it suitable for scenarios such as robot vision, drone aerial photography, and automotive surround-view systems.
