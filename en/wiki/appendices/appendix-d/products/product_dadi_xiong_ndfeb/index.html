Product Overview

Earth-Panda's high-performance sintered NdFeB magnets adopt the Nd₂Fe₁₄B-based sintered magnet technology route, covering common grades such as N, M, H, SH, UH, and EH. They can be customized into complex magnetic circuit shapes like arc segments, blocks, and rings according to customer drawings. The company has production capacity in Hefei, Baotou, and other locations, and has established a German subsidiary to expand European automotive customers.

The products feature high remanence, high maximum energy product, and high coercivity, and are widely used in new energy vehicle drive motors, wind turbine generators, industrial servo motors, and humanoid robot joint actuators.

Selection and Deployment Recommendations

  • For humanoid robot joint motors, it is recommended to prioritize evaluating the high-temperature stability of SH/UH and above grades.
  • The surface protection of the magnet must match the humidity and corrosion risk of the motor's operating environment.
