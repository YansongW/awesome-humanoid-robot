Product Overview

Wuzhou Xinchun angular contact ball bearings can simultaneously withstand radial and axial combined loads, achieving high rigidity and high-speed rotational support by adjusting the contact angle. The product uses bearing steel GCr15 material, processed through quenching, grinding, and superfinishing, with precision grades up to P5/P4. It is widely used in machine tool spindles, automotive wheel hubs, motors, and robot joint spindles.

Leveraging the full industry chain advantage of bearing rings, the company can provide customized bearing solutions for robot reducers, servo motors, and joint modules, and is expanding into thin-wall bearings dedicated to harmonic reducers and ball screw nuts.

Selection and Deployment Recommendations

  • When mounting in pairs, pay attention to the pairing method (DB/DF/DT) to ensure axial rigidity and load distribution.
  • Preload should be selected comprehensively based on speed, temperature rise, and rigidity requirements; excessive preload will increase heat generation.
  • For robot joint applications, it is recommended to choose P5 or higher precision and use clean lubricating grease.
