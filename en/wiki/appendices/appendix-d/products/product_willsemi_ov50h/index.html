Product Overview

The OmniVision OV50H is a 50-megapixel image sensor designed for the main rear camera of high-end smartphones. It utilizes PureCel®Plus-S stacked technology with 1.2 µm pixels, supports Dual Conversion Gain (DCG™) HDR, and Horizontal/Vertical Quad Phase Detection (H/V QPD) autofocus, delivering flagship-level low-light and focusing performance.
