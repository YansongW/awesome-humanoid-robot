Product Overview

High load capacity, compact structure, long service life, customizable bearings for robot joints and reducers. This series of products is launched by Nanfang Precision, primarily targeting the bearing / precision components / automotive parts market, with core parameters such as 5–60 mm, suitable for robots, automation equipment, and precision transmission scenarios.

As one of Nanfang Precision's representative products, needle roller bearings are widely used in automotive, industrial, and other fields. The product adopts mature manufacturing processes and can provide customized interfaces, wiring methods, and control protocols according to customer requirements.
