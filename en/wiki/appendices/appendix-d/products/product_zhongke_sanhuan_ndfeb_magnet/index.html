Product Overview

Zhongke Sanhuan's high-performance sintered NdFeB magnets adopt the Nd₂Fe₁₄B-based sintered magnet technology route, covering common grades such as N, M, H, SH, UH, and EH. Custom shapes like arc segments, blocks, and rings can be produced according to customer drawings for complex magnetic circuits. The product features high remanence, high maximum energy product, and high coercivity, and is widely used in new energy vehicle drive motors, industrial servo motors, wind turbines, and humanoid robot joint actuators.

Humanoid robot joint motors commonly use high-grade NdFeB magnets to achieve high torque density and high dynamic response. Leveraging its background from the Chinese Academy of Sciences and grain boundary diffusion technology, Zhongke Sanhuan holds a core position in the supply chain for this material.

Selection and Deployment Recommendations

  • For robot joint motor selection, comprehensively evaluate operating temperature, demagnetization curve, and magnetic circuit structure; prioritize grades SH/UH or above.
  • Surface protection of the magnet must match the humidity and corrosion risks of the motor's operating environment.
