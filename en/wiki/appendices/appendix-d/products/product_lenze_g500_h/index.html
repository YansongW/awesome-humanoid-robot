Product Overview

The Lenze g500-H is a series of coaxial helical gearboxes, featuring 2-stage or 3-stage helical gear design, with rated torque covering 45 to 20,000 N·m and reduction ratios up to approximately i=370. This series works seamlessly with Lenze m850 servo motors, i950 inverters, and FAST application software to form a complete motion control axis.

The g500-H offers various mounting options including foot, flange, solid shaft, and hollow shaft, with a maximum protection rating of IP65. The lifetime lubrication design reduces maintenance requirements, making it suitable for robotics, packaging, logistics, and machine tool applications.
