Product Overview

High degree of freedom, integrated micro drive unit, expandable tactile and force control, suitable for fine manipulation of humanoid robots. This series of products is launched by Inspire Robots, mainly targeting the dexterous hand / micro linear actuator / joint module market, with core parameters such as 6 active + 6 passive / 11 active (optional), applicable to robots, automation equipment, and precision transmission scenarios.

As one of Inspire Robots' representative products, the RH56DFX series dexterous hand is widely used in humanoid robots, prosthetics, and other fields. The product adopts mature manufacturing processes and can provide customized interfaces, wiring methods, and control protocols according to customer needs.
