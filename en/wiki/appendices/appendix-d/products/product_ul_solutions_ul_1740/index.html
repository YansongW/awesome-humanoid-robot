Product Overview

UL 1740 is a North American industrial robot safety standard published by UL Solutions, specifying safety construction, installation, operation, and maintenance requirements for industrial robots and robot systems. This standard is harmonized with ANSI/RIA R15.06 and ISO 10218, widely adopted by North American insurance, regulatory, and market entities, and serves as an important safety basis for industrial robots entering the U.S. and Canadian markets.

Application Scenarios

  • North American Market Launch for Industrial Robots: Meets OSHA, NRTL, and insurance requirements.
  • Collaborative Robot Work Cells: Combined with ISO/TS 15066 for force/speed limitation and safety function verification.
  • Robot System Integration: Evaluates overall safety of robots with peripheral equipment and safeguards.
  • Humanoid Robot Industrial Applications: Serves as a reference standard for North American market safety assessment.

Selection and Deployment Recommendations

  • Robots sold in North America should simultaneously reference UL 1740, ANSI/RIA R15.06, and NFPA 79.
  • Collaborate with UL Solutions for pre-assessment to clarify the NRTL certification path.
  • Prepare risk assessment, safety function descriptions, and type test reports.
