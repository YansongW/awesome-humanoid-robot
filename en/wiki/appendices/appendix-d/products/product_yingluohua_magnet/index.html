Product Overview

Yingluohua sintered NdFeB magnets adopt the Nd₂Fe₁₄B-based sintered magnet technology route, covering common grades such as N, M, H, SH, etc. They can be customized into arc, block, ring, and other magnetic circuit shapes according to customer drawings. Leveraging the rare earth resources and manufacturing base of Hengdian Group, the products are widely used in automotive, home appliances, consumer electronics, and industrial automation fields.

The company also has a micro-special motor business, allowing the magnet products to synergize with downstream small motors, serving lightweight, high-integration scenarios such as robot end effectors and dexterous hands.

Application Scenarios

  • New Energy Vehicles: Magnets for auxiliary motors such as windows, seats, and air conditioning.
  • Home Appliances: Magnets for motors in inverter air conditioners, washing machines, and vacuum cleaners.
  • Consumer Electronics: Magnets for vibration motors and micro motors.
  • Humanoid Robots: Magnetic circuits for lightweight micro drives in dexterous hands and end effectors.

Procurement and Deployment Suggestions

  • For lightweight robot end effectors, cost-effective grades such as N45/N48 can be prioritized.
  • For high-temperature joint motors, it is recommended to select SH grade or above and verify the demagnetization curve.
