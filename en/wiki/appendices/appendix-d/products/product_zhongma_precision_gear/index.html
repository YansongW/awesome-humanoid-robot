Product Overview

Zhongma Precision Transmission Gears are made of high-quality gear steel through forging, turning, hobbing/shaping, heat treatment, and finishing processes, with tooth profile accuracy reaching DIN 6–8. The product range covers cylindrical gears, planetary gears, input shafts, and reducer gear assemblies, widely used in automotive transmissions, new energy vehicle reducers, and robot joint reducers.

The company has complete gear processing and heat treatment capabilities and can provide different heat treatment solutions such as carburizing and quenching or induction hardening according to customer requirements, meeting high load capacity, low noise, and long service life demands.

Selection and Deployment Recommendations

  • When selecting, determine the module, tooth count, accuracy grade, and heat treatment process based on torque, speed, and noise requirements.
  • For high-load robot joints, carburized and quenched gears are recommended, along with tooth profile modification to reduce meshing noise.
  • Before assembly, check the gear pair backlash and contact pattern to ensure reliable lubrication system oil supply.
