Product Overview

SGS Robotics Testing and Certification Service covers the entire lifecycle of industrial robots, collaborative robots, service robots, and humanoid robots, providing electrical safety, mechanical safety, electromagnetic compatibility, radio frequency, functional safety, software and cybersecurity, environmental reliability, and global market access certification. The service covers pre-testing during the R&D phase, formal certification, factory audits, and ongoing surveillance, helping customers shorten time-to-market and reduce compliance risks.

Application Scenarios

  • Consumer Robot Launch: Electrical/EMC/Wireless certification for robotic vacuum cleaners, educational robots, etc.
  • Industrial Robot Export: CE, UKCA, North American market access and compliance documentation support.
  • Collaborative Robot Safety Verification: ISO/TS 15066 and ISO 10218 gap analysis and testing.
  • Humanoid Robot Full-Chain Compliance: Safety, functional safety, and cybersecurity assessment from joints and controllers to the complete machine.

Selection and Deployment Recommendations

  • Plan the certification path in advance based on the target market to avoid duplicate testing.
  • Use SGS pre-testing services to identify potential compliance issues during the design phase.
  • Prepare a complete BOM, circuit diagrams, risk analysis, and software documentation to accelerate certification.
