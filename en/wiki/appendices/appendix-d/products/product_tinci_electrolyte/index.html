Product Overview

Tinci Power Battery Electrolyte is composed of carbonate solvents, lithium hexafluorophosphate (LiPF₆) lithium salt, and functional additives. It features high ionic conductivity, a wide electrochemical window, excellent high and low temperature performance, and long cycle stability. Leveraging the integrated advantage of self-produced LiPF₆ and LiFSI, the company can provide formulations for high-nickel, high-voltage, fast-charging, and wide-temperature-range battery systems.

Electrolyte is one of the four main materials of lithium-ion batteries, directly affecting battery rate performance, cycle life, and safety. Humanoid robots, unmanned vehicles, and other highly dynamic mobile platforms have increased requirements for fast charging, wide temperature range, and high safety performance in batteries. Tinci's electrolyte occupies a core upstream position in this supply chain.

Selection and Deployment Recommendations

  • For robot batteries requiring low-temperature startup capability, a wide-temperature-range electrolyte formulation should be selected and matched with the positive and negative electrodes.
  • For fast-charging scenarios, electrolyte additives (such as FEC, LiFSI) should be optimized to reduce the risk of lithium plating.
