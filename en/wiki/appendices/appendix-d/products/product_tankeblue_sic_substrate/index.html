Product Overview

The TanKeBlue 6-inch conductive 4H-SiC substrate is grown using the PVT (Physical Vapor Transport) method and serves as the core foundational material for manufacturing power devices such as SiC MOSFETs and SiC SBDs. The product features high critical breakdown electric field, high thermal conductivity, and high electron saturation drift velocity, significantly enhancing the efficiency and power density of power devices.

Humanoid robots and new energy vehicle electric drive systems have clear demands for high-efficiency, high-power-density motor controllers. SiC devices can significantly reduce switching losses and thermal dissipation pressure. TanKeBlue SiC substrates are positioned at the very top of this power semiconductor supply chain.

Application Scenarios

  • New Energy Vehicles: SiC MOSFETs used in main drive inverters, OBC, DC-DC converters.
  • Photovoltaic Energy Storage: Power devices for photovoltaic inverters and energy storage converters.
  • Rail Transit: High-power devices for traction converters.
  • Robotics/Unmanned Platforms: High-power joint motor drivers for humanoid robots, electric drive systems for unmanned vehicles.
