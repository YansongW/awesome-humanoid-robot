Product Overview

Cycloidal pinwheel transmission, multi-tooth meshing, high rigidity, impact resistance, suitable for heavy-load, high-reliability industrial scenarios. This series is launched by Sumitomo Heavy Industries, primarily targeting the reducer / heavy machinery / precision transmission market, with core parameters such as 6:1 – 658,503:1, applicable to industrial robots, construction machinery, and humanoid robot scenarios.

As one of Sumitomo Heavy Industries' representative products, the Cyclo series cycloidal reducer is widely used in industrial robot bases, humanoid robot torso joints, mining machinery, and other fields. The product adopts mature manufacturing processes and can provide customized reduction ratios, lubrication methods, and mounting interfaces according to customer requirements.
