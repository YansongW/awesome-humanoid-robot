Product Overview

The LEONI Dacar® 302 Automotive Ethernet Cable is a single-pair Ethernet data cable launched by LEONI for in-vehicle/robot sensor networks, cameras, and LiDAR. This product features standardized interfaces, stable contact, and high-reliability industrial-grade design to meet the long-term stable requirements of robot systems for signal, power, or data connections.

Selection and Deployment Recommendations

  • Before selection, confirm the number of pins, current, voltage, protection level, and installation space. Request the official datasheet and samples if necessary.
  • For robot applications, focus on evaluating vibration resistance, bending resistance, oil resistance, and EMC shielding requirements. Prototype verification is recommended.
  • For large-volume procurement, it is recommended to confirm delivery time, MOQ, and RoHS/REACH compliance certificates through authorized distributors or the original manufacturer.
