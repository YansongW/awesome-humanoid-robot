Product Overview

Multi-tooth meshing between cycloidal gear and pin housing, high rigidity, high torque, suitable for heavy-load joints of industrial robots and humanoid robot torsos. This series is launched by Nantong Zhenkang, mainly targeting the RV reducer / welding equipment / motor market, with core parameters such as 30:1 – 192.4:1, applicable to industrial robots, humanoid robots, and welding automation scenarios.

As one of the representative products of Nantong Zhenkang, the RV-E series reducer is widely used in industrial robot bases/shoulders/waists, humanoid robot hip/knee joints, welding robots, and other fields. The product adopts mature manufacturing processes and can provide customized reduction ratios, output flanges, and lubrication configurations according to customer requirements.
