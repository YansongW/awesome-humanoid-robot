Product Overview

The 300M series is Bonfiglioli's flagship industrial planetary gearbox, offering both coaxial and right-angle configurations. The torque range covers 1,000 to 450,000 N·m, with reduction ratios up to 5,000:1. The product features a ductile iron housing, hardened sun/planet gears, and a modular input interface, compatible with IEC/NEMA motors or hydraulic motors.

This series offers 20 sizes, multiple output shaft options (solid keyed shaft, splined shaft, hollow shaft with shrink disc), and IP65/ATEX/cooling options. It is widely used in wind power, mining, construction machinery, and heavy-duty joints of humanoid robots.
