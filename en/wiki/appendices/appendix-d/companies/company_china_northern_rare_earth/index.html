Company Profile

China Northern Rare Earth is one of the world's largest suppliers of light rare earths. Leveraging the rare earth resource advantages of Baotou's Bayan Obo deposit, it has built a full industry chain layout from rare earth concentrates, smelting and separation to functional materials.

The company's main products include rare earth concentrates, rare earth oxides, rare earth metals, and magnetic material raw materials such as NdFeB strip-casting alloys. High-performance NdFeB permanent magnets are key materials for servo motors and frameless torque motors in humanoid robots. China Northern Rare Earth holds an important strategic position in the upstream raw material segment.

Technical Highlights: The strip-casting process refines grains and improves magnetic performance consistency, serving as a precursor material for high-performance sintered NdFeB.

Application Scenarios: Drive motors for new energy vehicles, wind turbine generators, and magnetic steels for joint motors in humanoid robots.

Relevance to Humanoid Robots

  • Batteries, power semiconductors, and advanced materials are common foundations for achieving long endurance, high dynamics, and lightweight design in humanoid robots.
  • Industrial robots and automated production lines provide reusable manufacturing capabilities for the assembly, testing, and mass production of humanoid robots.
