Company Profile

Taung Intelligence Technology was founded in 2020 as a national high-tech enterprise, focusing on the R&D, production, and sales of commercial and industrial cleaning robots. The company is committed to driving industry transformation with automated cleaning equipment, applying computational neural algorithms to visual navigation technology, and using low-computation, high-performance brain-inspired algorithms to create floor cleaning robots that "even cleaning staff can use."

Taung Intelligence pioneered the world's first visual and laser fusion navigation and positioning system, self-developing sensors such as 3D-ToF depth cameras, high-speed single-line LiDAR, and single-point lasers, significantly reducing robot manufacturing and maintenance costs. The company's products are widely used in transportation hubs and commercial complexes such as Shenzhen North Station and Raffles City, and it has become one of China's representative enterprises for "Intelligent Manufacturing" going global.
