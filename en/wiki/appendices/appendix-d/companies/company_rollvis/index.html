Company Profile

A world-leading professional manufacturer of planetary roller screws, renowned for high load capacity, high precision, and long service life.

Rollvis SA has long focused on the R&D and manufacturing of planetary roller screws. Its products are widely used in aerospace, machine tools, medical equipment, injection molding, robotics, and defense equipment. The R Series planetary roller screws are known for high speed, high acceleration, and compact structure, making them a key imported component source for high-load linear joints in humanoid robots.
