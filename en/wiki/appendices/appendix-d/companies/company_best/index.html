Company Profile

A domestic supplier of precision components and ball screws, providing high-precision transmission parts for automobiles, industrial equipment, and humanoid robots.

Best Precision Machinery primarily manufactures automotive turbocharger components, workholding fixtures, aircraft fuselage parts, and precision transmission components. Leveraging its precision machining capabilities, the company has expanded into ball screws, planetary roller screws, and linear guides, offering core linear transmission components for humanoid robots.
