Company Profile

Zhongke Sanhuan is one of the earliest enterprises in China with a university/research institute background to achieve industrialization of NdFeB permanent magnet materials. Leveraging the technical accumulation of the Institute of Physics, Chinese Academy of Sciences and Sanhuan New Materials Company, it has long been deeply engaged in sintered and bonded NdFeB magnets.

The company's product line covers high-performance magnetic steels and magnetic assemblies required for new energy vehicle drive motors, wind power generation, energy-saving home appliances, consumer electronics, industrial servo motors, and robot motors. Humanoid robot joint motors have a clear demand for magnetic steels with high magnetic energy product and high coercivity. Relying on its capabilities in grain boundary diffusion, grain refinement, and magnetic circuit design, Zhongke Sanhuan occupies a core midstream position in this material supply chain.
