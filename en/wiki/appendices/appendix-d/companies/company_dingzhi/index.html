Company Profile

Focuses on precision micro stepper motors, linear actuators, and ball/trapezoidal lead screws, providing linear joint components for humanoid robots.

Dingzhi Technology primarily manufactures precision stepper motors, lead screw motors, coreless motors, precision gearboxes, and linear actuators. Its products are widely used in medical equipment, industrial automation, automotive, and humanoid robots. Its micro lead screws and linear actuators can be applied to robot dexterous hands, linear joints, and precision positioning mechanisms.
