Company Profile

A leading domestic enterprise in elevators and precision transmission components, with batch supply capability for ball screws and guide rails.

Shanghai Electromechanical's main businesses include elevator manufacturing, elevator parts, printing and packaging machinery, hydraulic and pneumatic products, and precision transmission components. Its subsidiaries, Shanghai Mitsubishi Elevator and Nabtesco (China) Precision Machinery Co., Ltd., possess technical and capacity advantages in products such as reducers, ball screws, and guide rails, which are widely used in elevators, machine tools, automation equipment, and linear motion joints of humanoid robots.
