Company Profile

A leading domestic enterprise in linear drive systems, with products extending from home and medical applications to humanoid robots and industrial actuators.

Kaidi Electrical primarily focuses on linear drive systems, electric actuators, lifting columns, servo motors, and controllers, widely used in smart homes, smart offices, medical rehabilitation, and automotive sectors. In recent years, the company has actively deployed linear joint modules for humanoid robots, developing high thrust density electric cylinders and micro ball screw actuators.
