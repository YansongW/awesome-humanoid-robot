Company Profile

Bonfiglioli is a well-known Italian supplier of industrial transmission and drive technology, with products covering planetary gearboxes, helical/bevel helical gear motors, frequency inverters, and mobile machinery drive solutions. Its 300M series planetary gearboxes are renowned for high torque density and modularity.

The company's products are widely used in wind power, material handling, construction machinery, food and beverage, and robotics, providing localized support through a global distribution network.

Technical Highlights: Modular design, high torque density, right-angle and coaxial versions available, built-in cooling and multiple seals, compatible with various motors.

Application Scenarios: Wind turbine pitch control, mining machinery, industrial robot bases, humanoid robot torso joints, conveying and lifting equipment.

Other Representative Products

The F series helical gear motor can achieve torque up to tens of thousands of N·m; the Active Cube 8 inverter supports multi-axis servo control; mobile machinery wheel drives are used for AGVs/forklifts.
