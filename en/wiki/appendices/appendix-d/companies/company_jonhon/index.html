Company Profile

A leading enterprise in China's high-end connector and interconnection technology, with products covering defense, communications, new energy vehicles, and industrial equipment.

JONHON, a subsidiary of AVIC, is one of the largest and most technologically capable connector suppliers in China. The company provides electrical connectors, optical connectors, fluid connectors, and cable assemblies. In recent years, it has accelerated its expansion into high-voltage connectors for new energy vehicles, rail transit connectors, and robot interconnection solutions.
