Company Overview

Youibot is a leading industrial mobile robot company in China, focusing on AMR solutions for semiconductor, energy & power, and smart manufacturing scenarios.

Youibot offers latent-type, forklift-type, collaborative composite, and inspection AMRs, emphasizing SLAM navigation, dispatching systems, and MES/WMS integration capabilities. Its products have been deployed in semiconductor wafer fabs, power plant inspections, and automotive parts logistics, providing mobile platforms and dispatching expertise for factory automation beyond humanoid robots.
