Company Profile

Global pioneer in linear motion systems, inventor of the LM Guide (linear guide) and industry standard setter.

THK developed the world's first linear motion guide (LM Guide) in 1972, laying the foundation for modern rolling linear motion technology. The company's product range covers LM Guides, Ball Screws, Actuators, Cross Roller Guides, Smart Joint Modules, etc., widely used in machine tools, semiconductors, robotics, automotive, and medical equipment.
