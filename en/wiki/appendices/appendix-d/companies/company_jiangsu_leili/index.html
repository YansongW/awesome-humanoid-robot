Company Profile

A domestic supplier of micro motors and precision motion control solutions, covering home appliances, automotive, medical, and robotics sectors.

Jiangsu Leili focuses on the R&D and manufacturing of micro special motors and their components. Main products include stepper motors, synchronous motors, DC brushed/brushless motors, pumps, valves, and precision reduction mechanisms. The company continues to invest in coreless motors, brushless motors, and integrated joint modules, with products entering the supply chains of humanoid robots, smart vehicles, and medical devices.
