Company Profile

Global leader in magnesium alloy die-casting and automotive wheels, with an international footprint established through the acquisition of Canada's Meridian.

Wanfeng Auto Wheels specializes in automotive metal lightweight components, including magnesium alloy die-castings, aluminum alloy wheels, high-strength steel stampings, and coating services. Its subsidiary Meridian is one of the world's largest magnesium alloy die-casting companies, covering products such as automotive instrument panels, seat frames, and front-end modules. It is actively expanding into the humanoid robot magnesium alloy structural parts market.
