Company Profile

Lingbao CASBOT is a humanoid robot brand under Beijing Zhongke Huiling. The team comes from institutions such as the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Tsinghua University, and Beijing Institute of Technology.

The company proposes "Starting with the end in mind, born to be human," emphasizing the brain-inspired intelligence and multi-scenario deployment capability of CASBOT 01. It has established cooperation in fields such as aerospace and navigation, emergency rescue, underground operations, industrial manufacturing, and commercial services.
