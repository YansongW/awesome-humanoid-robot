Company Overview

Pudu Robotics is a globally renowned service robot company, known for the design of delivery robots such as BellaBot and KettyBot, with products sold in over a hundred countries worldwide.

Pudu Robotics focuses on delivery and cleaning robots for scenarios such as restaurants, hotels, office buildings, and hospitals. Its products feature bionic appearance, multimodal interaction, and autonomous navigation. The DINERBOT, BellaBot, and KettyBot series have high brand recognition in the global restaurant and hotel markets, providing references for the interaction design of humanoid robots in service scenarios.
