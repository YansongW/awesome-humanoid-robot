Company Profile

Betop Technology (Dalian Betop Culture Technology Co., Ltd.) is a high-tech cultural tourism overall solution provider with "technology + art + IP" as its core competitiveness. Founded in 2005, originating from the Multimedia Research Center of Dalian University of Technology, it is one of the earliest companies in China to deploy cultural tourism technology and the first cultural technology company to be listed on the NEEQ.

The company pioneered the field of mechanical giant art installations in China, creating a series of giant bionic mechanical art installations such as "China Giant Horse," "Jinling Bixie," "Golden Armor Qilin," and "Giant Bear Beibei." At WAIC 2025, Betop Technology globally premiered the "Giant God AI Super Agent," featuring the 4-meter-tall AI bionic giant beast "Bear Beibei" as its core exhibit, showcasing speech recognition, emotion analysis, memory learning, and multimodal interaction capabilities, propelling mechanical giants from "moving machines" to "emotional life forms."
