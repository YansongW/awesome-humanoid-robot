Company Profile

A leading domestic hydraulic component manufacturer, recently expanding into precision transmission and humanoid robot actuators.

Hengli Hydraulic is a globally leading supplier of hydraulic cylinders, hydraulic pumps and valves, hydraulic systems, and precision castings. Its products are widely used in construction machinery, agricultural machinery, metallurgy, marine, and new energy. Leveraging its precision manufacturing capabilities, the company has deployed linear transmission products such as ball screws, roller screws, and electric cylinders, offering high-load, high-response actuation solutions for humanoid robots.
