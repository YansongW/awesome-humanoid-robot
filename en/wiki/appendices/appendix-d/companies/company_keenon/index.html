Company Overview

Keenon Robotics is a leading service robot enterprise in China, specializing in delivery and reception robots for dining, hotel, hospital, and office scenarios.

Keenon Robotics' T-series delivery robots, W-series guide robots, and cleaning robots have been deployed in dozens of countries worldwide. Its products emphasize autonomous navigation, multi-robot scheduling, and open tray design, enabling point-to-point delivery tasks within buildings and providing commercial operation and scheduling experience for the deployment of humanoid robots in service scenarios.
