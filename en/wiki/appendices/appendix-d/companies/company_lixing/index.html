Company Profile

The largest specialized manufacturer of precision bearing steel balls in China, with products covering bearings, automobiles, precision instruments, and other fields.

Lixing Stock primarily manufactures bearing steel balls, stainless steel balls, ceramic balls, and precision rolling elements, with a size range covering micro balls to large balls. The company possesses core technology for material modification and strengthening. Its products are supplied to international bearing giants such as SKF, Schaeffler, and NSK, as well as major domestic bearing manufacturers, making it a key upstream material/rolling element supplier for the humanoid robot bearing industry chain.
