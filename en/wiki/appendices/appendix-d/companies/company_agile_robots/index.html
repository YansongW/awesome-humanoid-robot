Company Profile

An intelligent robot enterprise originating from the German Aerospace Center, focusing on the deep integration of AI and robotics.

Based on the technology of the German Aerospace Center's Institute of Robotics and Mechatronics (DLR/RMC), Agile Robots holds leading advantages in robot perception, force control systems, vision systems, operating systems, and AI deep learning. The company's products include a humanoid five-fingered intelligent dexterous hand, the seven-axis force-controlled robotic arm Diana series, autonomous mobile robots, and the full-size humanoid robot Agile ONE. Its business covers major global markets such as automotive, consumer electronics, and healthcare.
