Company Profile

Ningbo Yunsheng is one of the core suppliers of high-performance rare earth permanent magnet materials in China, covering NdFeB magnetic materials, servo motors, and intelligent equipment. The company has deployed magnetic material production capacity in Ningbo, Baotou, and other locations, with products primarily consisting of high-grade sintered NdFeB.

In response to the trend of humanoid robots, Ningbo Yunsheng's high-performance magnetic steel and servo motors can respectively address the magnetic circuit requirements of joint motors and the actuator body needs. The company possesses a complete process chain from magnetic powder preparation, molding and sintering to surface treatment, and supplies in bulk to new energy vehicles, industrial automation, wind power, and other fields.
