Company Profile

Yingluohua is a listed company under Hengdian Group, primarily engaged in rare earth magnetic materials, micro-special motors, and health equipment. The magnetic materials segment mainly produces sintered and bonded NdFeB, while the motor segment covers DC motors, stepper motors, servo motors, etc.

Leveraging the rare earth resources and manufacturing base of Hengdian Group, Yingluohua has formed a certain synergy in the fields of magnetic materials and motors. Its products can be applied in new energy vehicles, consumer electronics, smart homes, industrial automation, and the magnetic circuits and actuators of humanoid robot motors.
