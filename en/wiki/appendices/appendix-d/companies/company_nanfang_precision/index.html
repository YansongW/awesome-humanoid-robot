Company Profile

A domestic supplier of needle roller bearings and precision components, with products covering automotive, motorcycle, industrial, and robotics fields.

Jiangsu Nanfang Precision focuses on precision components such as needle roller bearings, overrunning clutches, and one-way pulley assemblies, serving as a key supplier in the automotive, motorcycle, and industrial sectors. The company actively deploys bearings for robot reducers and joint bearings, providing high-reliability bearing solutions for the rotational and linear joints of humanoid robots.
