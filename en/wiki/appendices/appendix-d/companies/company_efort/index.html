Company Overview

EFORT, based on industrial robots, has entered the humanoid robot field through its subsidiary Qizhi Robot, validating the application of the "General Technology Base for Intelligent Robots" in ToC scenarios.

In February 2025, EFORT released the first-generation Yobot series humanoid robots: R1 (bipedal) and W1 (wheeled). R1 is used to demonstrate the general technology platform; W1 has 32 degrees of freedom and has been validated for material handling in automotive final assembly logistics. In February 2026, the company further launched the Yobot R2V1/R2V2 and W2 iterative products.
