Company Profile

A key domestic supplier of RV reducers and welding equipment, and one of the representative enterprises for domestic RV reducers.

Nantong Zhenkang started with welding materials and welding equipment, later entering the RV reducer field, becoming one of the first domestic companies to achieve mass production of RV reducers. The company's products include RV reducers, welding wire feeders, servo motors, and core components for welding robots. These products are widely used in industrial robots, humanoid robots, welding automation, and aerospace.
