Company Profile

A global leading supplier of gripping systems, clamping technology, and robot end-effectors.

Founded in 1945 and headquartered in Lauffen am Neckar, Germany, SCHUNK is a global leader in clamping technology and gripping systems. Its product portfolio covers machine tool chucks, automation grippers, collaborative robot end-effectors, and the SVH five-finger dexterous hand. The SCHUNK SVH is the world's first five-finger robotic hand to receive DGUV certification for collaborative operation, widely used in service robots, collaborative robots, and industrial assembly scenarios.
