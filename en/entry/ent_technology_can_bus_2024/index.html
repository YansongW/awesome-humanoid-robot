Definition and Positioning of CAN Bus

CAN Bus belongs to the technology type. Its domains include: software middleware, design engineering. Value chain level: intelligent layer, midstream. It is a fieldbus connecting the joint motor drives of a humanoid robot to the central controller. The English name is CAN Bus. The Korean name is CAN Bus.

The core mechanism of CAN Bus determines its performance boundaries in humanoid robot systems. Understanding its internal structure, signal flow, and control interfaces aids in system integration and optimization. Key Parameters and Selection Points

In engineering practice, selecting CAN Bus requires comprehensive consideration of performance indicators, reliability, cost, supply chain maturity, and compatibility with the overall system.
