Definition and Positioning of Product Liability

Product liability falls under the concept category. Its domain includes policy, regulation, and ethics. At the value chain level, it pertains to the verification and market layer. The legal responsibility of manufacturers and sellers for personal injury or property damage caused by defective or unsafe products directly applies to humanoid robots. The English term is Product Liability. The Korean term is 제품 책임.
