Definition and Positioning of de Leva Anthropometry

de Leva Anthropometry belongs to the Technology category. Its domains include: Design Engineering, Evaluation Benchmarks. Value chain level: Midstream, Validation Markets. It is a widely used anthropometric dataset and body segment parameter estimation method in biomechanical modeling, often applied to define reference human body mass, center of mass, and moments of inertia. The English name is de Leva Anthropometry. The Korean name is 드 레바 인체측정학.
