Summary

Deliver the Business Model Canvas along with at least three priority scenarios and their corresponding KPIs.

Overview

Phase/Work Package: Project Initiation and Business Baseline

Content

Methods/Tools: TAM/SAM/SOM Analysis, User Personas, Scenario Storyboards, Competitive Benchmarking (Boston Matrix)

Design Thinking Logic: Identify scenarios where robots create quantifiable economic value; scenarios determine form factor, pricing, and performance priorities.

Key Constraints: Budget ceiling, entry barriers, regulatory restrictions, customer willingness to pay.

Completion Criteria/Deliverables: Deliver a "Business Model Canvas" and at least 3 prioritized scenarios with corresponding KPIs.

Level-3 Subtasks and Level-4 Key Actions:

  • P0.1.1.1 Input Review and Target Quantification

  • Organize upstream inputs, reference standards, and resources required for the "Market Opportunity and Scenario Canvas"; convert completion criteria into quantifiable acceptance indicators; and assign Owner and milestones.

    • List all upstream inputs and confirm versions.
    • Convert acceptance criteria into quantifiable KPIs.
    • Establish task Owner, timeline, and risk register.

  • P0.1.1.2 Solution/Method Design

  • Develop implementation methods or candidate solutions for the "Market Opportunity and Scenario Canvas"; use "TAM/SAM/SOM Analysis, User Personas, Scenario Storyboards, Competitive Benchmarking (Boston Matrix)" for validation; clarify technical roadmap and resource requirements.

    • Generate at least 2 candidate solutions.
    • Establish an evaluation matrix and assign quantitative scores.
    • Organize review and freeze the solution.

  • P0.1.1.3 Implementation/Prototype/Sample Production

  • Execute the implementation of the "Market Opportunity and Scenario Canvas" according to the design plan; produce prototypes, samples, or complete key steps; record process data.

    • Build models/prototypes and record key parameters.
    • Perform simulation or prototype validation.
    • Record anomalies and deviations.

  • P0.1.1.4 Verification and Issue Closure

  • Verify the output of the "Market Opportunity and Scenario Canvas"; check if completion criteria are met; document issues and track until closure.

    • Develop test/review plan and pass criteria.
    • Execute tests and record raw data.
    • Output issue list and improvement measures.

  • P0.1.1.5 Documentation and Downstream Delivery

  • Output the final report/drawing/specification for the "Market Opportunity and Scenario Canvas"; update ICD/BOM/SOP/requirements traceability chain; complete formal delivery to downstream processes.
    • Write documents per template and cite raw data.
    • Complete internal review and version control.
    • Release and notify downstream dependents.
