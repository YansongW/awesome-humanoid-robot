Summary

A regularity condition on the constraints of an optimization problem that guarantees the validity of the Karush-Kuhn-Tucker necessary optimality conditions at a local optimum.

Sources

  • J. Nocedal and S. J. Wright, Numerical Optimization, 2nd ed., Springer, 2006
