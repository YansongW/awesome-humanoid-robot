Definition and Positioning of Robotis

Robotis is categorized as a component manufacturer. Its fields include components and design engineering. It operates at the upstream and midstream levels of the value chain. A South Korean company, it is famous for its Dynamixel smart actuators, which are extensively applied in research robots, educational platforms, and humanoid hands. The English name is Robotis.
