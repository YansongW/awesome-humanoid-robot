Definition and Positioning of NVIDIA Isaac Lab

NVIDIA Isaac Lab belongs to the software platform category. Its domains include software middleware, AI models, and algorithms. Its value chain level is the intelligence layer. It is a modular learning framework based on Isaac Sim for large-scale robot policy training. The English name is NVIDIA Isaac Lab. The Korean name is NVIDIA Isaac Lab.
