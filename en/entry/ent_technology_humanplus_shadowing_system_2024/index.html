Definition and Positioning of the HumanPlus Shadowing System

The HumanPlus Shadowing System belongs to the Technology category. Its domains include Data and Datasets, AI Models and Algorithms. Value chain level: Intelligence layer. It employs a low-level strategy of RGB camera-based human pose estimation combined with simulation training to achieve full-body humanoid robot teleoperation and data collection. Its English name is HumanPlus Shadowing System. Its Korean name is HumanPlus 섀도잉 시스템.
