Definition and Positioning of Robot-as-a-Service (RaaS)

Robot-as-a-Service (RaaS) belongs to the concept type. Its domains include: Applications and Markets. The value chain level is: Validation and Market Layer. A business model that provides robots through leasing or subscription rather than outright purchase, bundling maintenance, software updates, and fleet management. The English name is Robot-as-a-Service (RaaS). The Korean name is 로봇 as a Service(RaaS).
