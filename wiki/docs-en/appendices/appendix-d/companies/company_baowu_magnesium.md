## Company Overview

One of the world's largest magnesium alloy producers, focusing on magnesium/aluminum alloy materials, die-cast parts, and lightweight solutions.

Baowu Magnesium, formerly known as Yunhai Metals, is now controlled by China Baowu. The company has a complete industrial chain from dolomite mining and primary magnesium smelting to deep processing of magnesium alloys. Products include magnesium alloy ingots, magnesium alloy die-cast parts, aluminum alloy melting and die-cast parts, widely used in automotive, 3C, aerospace, and lightweight structural components for robotics.
