## Company Profile

A leading domestic manufacturer of precision aluminum alloy components for new energy vehicles, covering the three-electric system, chassis, and body structural parts.

Xusheng Group specializes in aluminum alloy and magnesium alloy die casting and precision machining. It is a core supplier to Tesla and other leading new energy vehicle companies. Its products include battery pack housings, motor housings, controller housings, and chassis structural parts. In recent years, it has expanded into lightweight components for humanoid robots, such as joint housings and torso structural parts.
