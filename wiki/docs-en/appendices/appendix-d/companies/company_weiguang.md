## Company Profile

A leading domestic manufacturer of cold chain motors and fans, with deep expertise in external rotor motor and EC fan technology.

Weiguang focuses on the R&D and manufacturing of refrigeration motors, freezer motors, external rotor fans, and EC fans. Its products are widely used in cold chain logistics, HVAC, telecommunications, power, and new energy vehicles. The company has advantages in high-efficiency motor design, electronic control, and system integration. Its products can provide thermal management, heat dissipation, and ventilation solutions for complete robots.
