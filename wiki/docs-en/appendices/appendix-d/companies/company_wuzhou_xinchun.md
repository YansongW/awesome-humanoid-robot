## Company Profile

A core domestic supplier of bearing rings and precision bearings, with products covering automotive, wind power, construction machinery, and robotics sectors.

Wuzhou Xinchun primarily manufactures bearing rings, finished bearings, automotive components, and precision forgings. It is a ring supplier for globally renowned bearing manufacturers. The company possesses full-chain capabilities in forging, turning, heat treatment, and grinding. In recent years, it has actively expanded into precision components such as robot reducer bearings and lead screw nuts.
