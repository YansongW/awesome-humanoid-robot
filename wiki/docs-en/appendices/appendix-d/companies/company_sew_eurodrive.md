## Company Profile

SEW-EURODRIVE is one of the global leaders in drive technology, offering a modular product portfolio ranging from gearmotors, servo motors, and frequency inverters to complete automation solutions. Its products are renowned for high reliability, maintainability, and a global service network.

In the servo field, the company offers the PS.F and PS.C series low-backlash planetary servo gearmotors, as well as helical/bevel servo gearmotors such as R..CMP, F..CMP, and K..CMP, covering a torque range from 50 N·m to 6,000 N·m.
