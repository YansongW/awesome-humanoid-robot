## Company Profile

Earth-Panda is a national high-tech enterprise specializing in the R&D, production, and sales of high-performance sintered NdFeB permanent magnet materials. The company has production bases in Hefei, Anhui, and Baotou, among other locations, and has established a German subsidiary to expand into the European market.

The company's products are primarily high-grade sintered NdFeB, widely used in new energy vehicle drive motors, wind power generation, energy-efficient home appliances, industrial automation, and consumer electronics. With the growing demand for high magnetic energy product magnets in humanoid robot joint motors, Earth-Panda occupies a midstream position in the material supply chain, leveraging its grain boundary diffusion technology and large-scale production capacity.
