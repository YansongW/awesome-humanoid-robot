## Company Profile

A leading enterprise in domestic precision CNC machine tools and rolling functional components, long-term undertaking national high-end manufacturing equipment research tasks.

Qinchuan Machine Tool Group focuses on gear processing machine tools, thread grinders, machining centers, and rolling functional components as its core business. Its subsidiary, Hanjiang Machine Tool, specializes in the R&D and manufacturing of ball screws, linear guides, and planetary roller screws. Leveraging the technical accumulation from the "04 Special Project," the company deploys high-precision transmission components in fields such as humanoid robots, aviation, aerospace, and automotive.
