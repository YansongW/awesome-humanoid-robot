## Company Profile

One of the oldest professional manufacturers of rolling function components in China, covering high-end machine tools, robots, and automation equipment.

Nanjing Craft Equipment Manufacturing Co., Ltd., formerly known as Nanjing Machine Tool Accessories Factory, has long focused on the R&D and production of rolling function components such as ball screw pairs, rolling linear guide pairs, and planetary roller screw pairs. The company possesses a complete process chain from raw material heat treatment, precision grinding to assembly and inspection, and is an important supplier for domestic substitution of high-end screw and guide components.
