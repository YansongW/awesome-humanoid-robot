## Company Profile

BTR is the global leader in lithium-ion battery anode materials. Its product portfolio includes artificial graphite, natural graphite, silicon-based anodes, high-nickel cathodes, and solid-state battery-related materials. The company has production capacity deployed in Shenzhen, Tianjin, Jiangsu, Sichuan, and multiple overseas locations.

Humanoid robots, quadruped robots, and unmanned vehicles have clear demands for high-energy-density, high-safety batteries. BTR's anode and cathode materials, as core materials for power batteries, occupy a key upstream position in the robot energy system.
