## Company Profile

Apex Dynamics is a leading Taiwanese manufacturer of precision planetary gearboxes, renowned for its AB, AF, and AE series high-torque, low-backlash planetary gearboxes. The company employs helical gears and ISO 9409-1 flange outputs, with products widely used in CNC, robotics, semiconductor, and automation equipment.

In addition to standard planetary gearboxes, Apex offers right-angle ABR/AER series, rotary tables, harmonic gearboxes, and customized gearboxes, serving global OEMs and system integrators.
