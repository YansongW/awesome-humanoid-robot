## Company Overview

Creator, manufacturer, and operator of cloud-intelligent robots.

Founded by Huang Xiaoqing in 2015, CloudMinds builds a full-stack cloud robot technology system centered on the vision of "Cloud-Intelligence, Connecting the Future," comprising a "Cloud Brain + Secure Network + Robot" architecture. The company has launched products such as the soft humanoid service robot Cloud Ginger, the bipedal humanoid robot XR-4, and cloud-based delivery and patrol robots, empowering robots with multimodal perception, cognition, and decision-making through the RobotGPT embodied intelligence large model.
