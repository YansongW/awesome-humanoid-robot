## Company Profile

A domestic professional manufacturer of automotive transmissions and gear transmission components, with capabilities in precision gear, shaft, and housing processing.

Zhongma Transmission primarily produces automotive transmission assemblies, gears, drive shafts, and new energy vehicle reducer components, supplying major domestic automotive and construction machinery manufacturers. The company possesses a complete process chain including gear machining, heat treatment, and assembly, and is expanding into robot joint reducer gears and precision structural parts.
