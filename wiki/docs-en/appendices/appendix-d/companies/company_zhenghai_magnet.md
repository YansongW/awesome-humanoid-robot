## Company Profile

Zhenghai Magnet is one of the representative enterprises in China's high-performance NdFeB permanent magnet material industry. Its main products are sintered NdFeB magnets and assemblies, widely used in new energy vehicles, wind power, energy-saving home appliances, industrial automation, and other fields.

The company has launched product lines such as "low-heavy rare earth magnets" and "ultra-light rare earth magnets," reducing dependence on scarce resources through grain boundary diffusion and heavy-rare-earth-free/low-heavy-rare-earth formulations. Humanoid robot joint motors have strong demand for high-torque-density, high-temperature-resistant magnetic steel, and Zhenghai Magnet's high-performance magnetic materials can directly enter this supply chain.
