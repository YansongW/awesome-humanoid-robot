## Company Profile

Kusa Technology is an innovative enterprise focused on the field of urban service robots, with sanitation scenarios as its first commercialization direction. The company's self-developed unmanned cleaning robots are based on an embodied robot technology architecture, integrating a multimodal perception system centered on AI vision. Leveraging automotive-grade product development processes and supply chain systems, they achieve centimeter-level edge-cleaning accuracy in open scenarios.

The company's products include the Kusa Xingyun® and Kusa Zhijuren® series of unmanned cleaning robots, along with an intelligent cloud control platform for efficient cluster coordination. As of 2026, Kusa's unmanned cleaning robots have been deployed in nearly 40 cities domestically and internationally, covering various open scenarios such as urban roads, parks and scenic areas, campuses and industrial parks, ports, airports, and high-speed rail stations. They have also been selected for the "Shanghai Intelligent Robot Top 100 Scenario Cases."
