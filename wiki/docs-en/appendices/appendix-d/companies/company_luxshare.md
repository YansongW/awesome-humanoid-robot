## Company Profile

A globally leading precision manufacturing platform enterprise, providing connectors, cable harnesses, and structural components for consumer electronics, automotive, communications, and robotics.

Luxshare Precision started with computer connectors and has now expanded to high-speed connectors, RF cable harnesses, automotive wiring harnesses, wireless charging modules, acoustic modules, and precision metal structural components. The company possesses capabilities in mold design, automated assembly, and vertical integration, and is expanding into automotive electronics and humanoid robot components.
