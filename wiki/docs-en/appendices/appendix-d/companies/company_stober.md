## Company Profile

STOBER is a German drive technology specialist offering high-precision planetary gearboxes, right-angle gearboxes, helical/bevel gear units, as well as STOBER servo motors and drives. Its products are known for U.S.-based assembly, fast delivery, and modular customization.

The product range covers the economy PE series, precision P/PH series, right-angle PK/PHK/PHQK series, and helical/bevel C/F/K series, providing complete drive shafts for robotics, machine tools, packaging, and food industries.
