## Company Profile

GW Composites is a leading domestic supplier of carbon fiber and composite materials. Its products cover carbon fiber tows, fabrics, prepregs, and composite finished products, widely used in aerospace, wind turbine blades, sports and leisure, automotive, and rail transit.

Carbon fiber composites feature high strength, high modulus, and low density, making them ideal materials for lightweight design of humanoid robot limb structures, shells, and joints.

**Application Scenarios**: Aerospace secondary structures, drone frames, humanoid robot limb shells and support structures.

## Relevance to Humanoid Robots

- Batteries, power semiconductors, and advanced materials are common foundations for achieving long endurance, high dynamics, and lightweight design in humanoid robots.
- Industrial robots and automated production lines provide reusable manufacturing capabilities for the assembly, testing, and mass production of humanoid robots.
