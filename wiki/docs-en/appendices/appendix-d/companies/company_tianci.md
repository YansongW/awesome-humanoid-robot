## Company Profile

Tinci Materials is the global leader in lithium-ion battery electrolytes, covering electrolytes, lithium hexafluorophosphate, LiFSI, additives, and cathode material precursors. The company has production capacity in Guangzhou, Jiujiang, Ningde, Nantong, and Europe.

Electrolyte is one of the four main materials of lithium-ion batteries, determining the battery's ionic conductivity, high and low temperature performance, and cycle life. As humanoid robots, unmanned vehicles, and other high-dynamic mobile platforms demand improved fast charging, wide temperature range, and safety performance, Tinci Materials' high-performance electrolytes occupy a core upstream position in this supply chain.
