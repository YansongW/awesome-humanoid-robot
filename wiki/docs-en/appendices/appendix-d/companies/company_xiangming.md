## Company Profile

A leading domestic enterprise in EC fans and intelligent motors, focusing on high efficiency, energy saving, and digital drives.

Xiangming Intelligent specializes in the R&D and production of EC (Electronically Commutated) motors, fans, and intelligent drive control systems. Its products are widely used in HVAC, cold chain, communications, new energy vehicles, and robot thermal management. The company possesses capabilities in motor design, electronic control, and system integration, providing high-efficiency cooling fans, servo fans, and customized drive solutions for robots.
