## Company Profile

HIWIN (HIWIN Technologies) is a globally leading linear motion control brand, offering ball screws, linear guides, linear motors, torque motors, direct drive motors, industrial robots, and medical robots. Its products are renowned for high precision, high rigidity, and high cost-effectiveness, widely used in CNC, semiconductor, automation, and robotics fields.

The company has a complete product line from C0 grade ground ball screws to C7 grade rolled ball screws, as well as HG, EG, MG series ball/roller linear guides, and can provide KK series linear modules and LMC linear motors.
