## Company Overview

Quicktron is one of the earliest companies in China focusing on warehouse logistics robots, offering products such as latent-type, bin-type, and heavy-duty AMRs.

Quicktron's M-series latent-type robots, C-series bin robots, and FlashPick solutions serve warehouse automation in industries such as e-commerce, pharmaceuticals, and manufacturing. Its intelligent warehouse operating system enables inventory optimization, path planning, and multi-robot coordination, providing an automation foundation for the warehousing and logistics segment of the humanoid robot supply chain.
