## Company Profile

An innovative enterprise focused on the R&D and manufacturing of dexterous hands and embodied intelligent robots.

RobotPlus focuses on the "RobotPlus Hand" series, covering four major categories: industrial parallel grippers, collaborative hands, bionic hands, and heavy-duty hands. It also provides flexible, safe, and intelligent robotic grasping and manipulation solutions for fields such as aerospace, industrial manufacturing, warehousing and logistics, education and research, and life sciences. The company emphasizes the integration of bionic structures and intelligent control algorithms, continuously promoting the commercial application of dexterous manipulation.
