## Company Profile

Festo is a global leader in pneumatic and electric automation technology and has a broad influence in the field of bionic robotics.

Festo offers cylinders, valve terminals, servo pneumatics, sensors, and automation platforms. Its Bionic Learning Network has developed flexible bionic robots such as the BionicSoftArm and BionicSoftHand. These products demonstrate the potential of pneumatic artificial muscles and human-robot collaboration, providing technical references for flexible actuation and grasping in humanoid robots.
