## Company Profile

A leading domestic company in the field of self-lubricating bearings, focusing on the R&D and manufacturing of metal-polymer composite plain bearings.

Changsheng Bearing primarily produces self-lubricating bearings, plain bearing bushings, composite bearings, and spherical plain bearings. Its products are widely used in automobiles, construction machinery, hydraulic systems, automation equipment, and robotics. The company possesses core process capabilities such as material formulation, sintering compounding, and precision forming, and can provide maintenance-free, low-friction, and wear-resistant bearing solutions.
