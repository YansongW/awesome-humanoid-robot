## Company Profile

Founded in 2016, Standard Robots is a globally leading provider of industrial intelligent mobile robot solutions, dedicated to enabling smart factories across various industrial scenarios. The company offers tailored one-stop robot solutions, including a core robotics technology platform, a versatile range of industrial intelligent robot products, and the all-in-one intelligent collaboration system RoboVerse, capable of scheduling over 2,000 robots in a single simulated scenario.

The company continuously invests in cutting-edge technologies such as SLAM navigation, multi-sensor fusion positioning, end-to-end navigation, multimodal perception, and Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models, and has independently developed the industrial intelligent mobile robot operating system SROS. In 2025, Standard Robots showcased the embodied intelligent robot DARWIN at WAIC 2025 and participated in factory and hotel scenario competitions at the world's first humanoid robot games.
