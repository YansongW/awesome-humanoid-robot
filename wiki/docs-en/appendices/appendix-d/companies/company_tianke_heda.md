## Company Profile

TanKeBlue is one of the earliest enterprises in China to industrialize silicon carbide (SiC) substrates, incubated by the SiC research team of the Institute of Physics, Chinese Academy of Sciences. The company specializes in conductive and semi-insulating SiC substrates, covering 4-inch and 6-inch products, and is advancing the development of 8-inch substrates.

SiC substrates are core materials for power semiconductors and RF devices, widely used in new energy vehicle electric drives, photovoltaic inverters, charging piles, power grids, and rail transit. Humanoid robot joint drives have a clear demand for efficient, high-power-density motor controllers, and SiC MOSFETs can significantly improve system efficiency. TanKeBlue is at the very upstream of this material supply chain.
