## Company Profile

Enjie Stock is the global leader in wet-process lithium battery separators. It integrated technology and capacity through the acquisition of Shanghai Enjie, with customers covering major domestic and international power battery manufacturers. The company has production bases in Yunnan, Shanghai, Jiangsu, Jiangxi, Hungary, and other locations.

Separators are one of the four main materials for lithium-ion batteries, directly affecting battery safety, energy density, and cycle life. Humanoid robots and unmanned platforms have clear demands for high-safety, high-energy-density batteries. Enjie Stock's high-end base films and coated separators occupy a core upstream position in this supply chain.
