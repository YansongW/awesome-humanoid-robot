## Company Profile

Global leader in micro drive systems, providing micro transmission solutions for consumer electronics, automotive, medical, and robotics.

Zhaowei Machinery & Electronics focuses on micro drive systems, with products including micro DC motors, precision gearboxes, linear drive modules, and micro joints. The company offers customized micro transmission solutions in the 3.4mm–38mm diameter range, with capabilities in mold design, gear processing, and automated assembly.
