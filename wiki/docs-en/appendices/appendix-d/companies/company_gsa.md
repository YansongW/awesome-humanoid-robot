## Company Profile

Swiss high-precision planetary roller screw and electromechanical actuator supplier, focusing on high-load, high-reliability linear transmission.

GSA SA specializes in planetary roller screws and their derived electric cylinders and servo actuators, covering aerospace, aviation, medical, robotics, machine tools, and nuclear industries. The company emphasizes full-process quality control from design, manufacturing to testing, and can provide customized solutions for small-batch, high-complexity applications.
