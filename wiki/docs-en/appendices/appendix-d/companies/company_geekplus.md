## Company Profile

Geek+ is a globally renowned provider of logistics robot solutions, known for its warehouse picking AMRs, sorting robots, and PopPick container systems.

Geek+'s P-series picking robots, S-series sorting robots, and RoboShuttle container robots have been deployed on a large scale in global e-commerce, retail, and pharmaceutical warehouses. Its Robot Management System (RMS) supports multi-brand device co-operation and intelligent scheduling, providing a logistics infrastructure reference for human-robot collaboration in warehouse scenarios involving humanoid robots.
