## Company Profile

Sanan Optoelectronics is a leading compound semiconductor enterprise in China, covering LED chips, SiC/GaN power semiconductors, RF devices, and optical communication chips. The company operates compound semiconductor production lines in Xiamen, Quanzhou, Changsha (Hunan), and others, and has deployed a full industrial chain for SiC substrates, epitaxy, devices, and packaging.

For humanoid robots and new energy vehicle electric drive systems, SiC MOSFETs and GaN HEMTs can provide motor control solutions with higher efficiency and higher power density. Sanan Optoelectronics' SiC epitaxial wafers and power devices occupy a core upstream position in this supply chain.
