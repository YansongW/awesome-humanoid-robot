## Company Overview

Magic Atom is a high-tech enterprise focused on the R&D, production, and industrial application of embodied intelligence and general-purpose humanoid robots. The company's predecessor can be traced back to the embodied intelligence and humanoid robot R&D team within Dreame Technology, and it was officially registered as an independent entity in January 2024. The core team comes from renowned domestic and international universities such as Tsinghua University, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Zhejiang University, Beihang University, and New York University, with R&D personnel accounting for over 80% of the workforce.

The company adheres to full-stack in-house development of both software and hardware. Its core hardware covers key components such as full joint modules, dexterous hands, reducers, and drivers, and it has established advantages in algorithm areas including motion control, navigation, multimodal perception, and embodied manipulation. Its product matrix includes the full-size general-purpose humanoid robot MagicBot Gen1, the high-dynamic small humanoid robot MagicBot Z1, and a series of quadruped robots. In 2026, Magic Atom became the strategic partner for intelligent robots at the CCTV Spring Festival Gala.
