## Company Profile

Shandong Tianyue is a leading domestic manufacturer of silicon carbide single crystal substrates, with products covering semi-insulating and conductive 4/6/8-inch SiC substrates, widely used in RF devices, power devices, and optoelectronic devices.

Silicon carbide substrates are the base material for power chips such as SiC MOSFETs and Schottky diodes. If the electric drive system of humanoid robots adopts SiC power devices, inverter efficiency and power density can be significantly improved. SICC holds a leading position in the domestic substrate material sector.
