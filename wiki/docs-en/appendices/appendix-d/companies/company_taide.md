## Company Profile

A domestic leader in automotive A/C compressor bearings, specializing in the R&D and manufacturing of high-precision rolling bearings.

Taide Stock primarily produces automotive A/C compressor bearings, tensioner bearings, clutch bearings, and precision ball bearings, supplying major domestic and international automotive A/C and engine system suppliers. The company possesses capabilities in precision grinding, superfinishing, and automated assembly. In recent years, it has expanded into high-value-added fields such as new energy vehicles and robot joint bearings.
