## Company Profile

JL MAG is a world-leading manufacturer of high-performance rare-earth permanent magnet materials. Its products are primarily sintered NdFeB, covering grades such as N, M, H, SH, UH, EH, and others.

The company's products are widely used in new energy vehicle drive motors, wind turbine generators, energy-efficient home appliances, and industrial robot servo motors. Humanoid robot joint motors commonly use high-grade NdFeB magnetic steel to achieve high torque density, and JL MAG occupies a core midstream position in this material supply chain.

**Technical Highlights**: Utilizes grain boundary diffusion and other technologies to enhance coercivity and temperature resistance; customizable in arc, block, ring, and other shapes, suitable for frameless torque motor rotors.

**Application Scenarios**: New energy vehicle drive motors, industrial robot servo motors, humanoid robot joint actuators.

## Relevance to Humanoid Robots

- Batteries, power semiconductors, and advanced materials are common foundations for achieving long endurance, high dynamics, and lightweight design in humanoid robots.
- Industrial robots and automated production lines provide reusable manufacturing capabilities for the assembly, testing, and mass production of humanoid robots.
