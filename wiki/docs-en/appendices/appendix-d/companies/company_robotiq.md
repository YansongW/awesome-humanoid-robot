## Company Overview

Global leader in adaptive grippers and end-effectors for collaborative robots.

Founded in 2008 and headquartered in Quebec City, Canada, Robotiq specializes in plug-and-play adaptive grippers, force control sensors, wrist cameras, and vacuum grippers for collaborative robots. The Robotiq 2F series two-finger grippers are among the best-selling grippers in the global collaborative robot market, supporting parallel grip, enveloping grip, and internal grip, and are compatible with mainstream collaborative robot arms such as Universal Robots.
