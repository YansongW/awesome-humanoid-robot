## Company Profile

A leading domestic high-end electric spindle manufacturer, expanding into torque motors, harmonic drives, and humanoid robot joint modules.

Hao Zhi Electromechanical started with CNC machine tool electric spindles and gradually expanded into linear motors, torque motors, harmonic drives, and robot joint modules. The company has accumulated technical expertise in high-speed, high-precision motors and precision transmission, providing integrated joints and core transmission components for humanoid robots.
