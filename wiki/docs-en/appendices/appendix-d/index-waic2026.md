# Appendix D.2 WAIC 2026 Exhibitor Index

The 2026 World Artificial Intelligence Conference (WAIC) was held in Shanghai from July 17–20, 2026. The exhibition area of this year's conference exceeded 100,000 square meters for the first time, with over 1,100 companies bringing more than 3,000 exhibits. Notably, **the two major tracks of intelligent computing and embodied intelligence each gathered over 200 companies**. Humanoid robots, AI dexterous hands, and core robot components became some of the biggest highlights.

> Data source: [Yicai WAIC 2026 Report](https://www.163.com/dy/article/L184MBN10519DDQ2.html), [Jufair Exhibitor List](https://www.jufair.com/information/66497.html).
