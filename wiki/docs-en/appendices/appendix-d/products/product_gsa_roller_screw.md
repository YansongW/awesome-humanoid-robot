## Product Overview

GSA planetary roller screws represent Swiss precision engineering, targeting high-demand applications such as aerospace, medical, nuclear industry, and humanoid robots. GSA not only offers standard planetary roller screws but can also integrate the screw with servo motors and encoders to form a fully integrated electromechanical actuator (EMA).

Its products are known for high dynamic response, strong customization capabilities, and high reliability, making it a preferred supplier for small-batch, high-complexity projects.
