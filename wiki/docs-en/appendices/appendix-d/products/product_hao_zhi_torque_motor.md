## Product Overview

Direct-drive with zero backlash, high rigidity, and high torque density, suitable for direct drive in robot joints and precision rotary tables. This series is launched by Hao Zhi Electromechanical, primarily targeting the spindle motor / torque motor / harmonic reducer / joint module market, with core parameters such as 5–200 N·m, applicable to robots, automation equipment, and precision transmission scenarios.

As one of the representative products of Hao Zhi Electromechanical, the torque motor is widely used in CNC, engraving and milling, grinding, and other fields. The product adopts mature manufacturing processes and can provide customized interfaces, wiring methods, and control protocols according to customer requirements.
