## Product Overview

The planetary roller screw launched by Hanjiang Machine Tool, a subsidiary of Qinchuan Machine Tool, is designed for high-load, high-precision linear transmission scenarios. Leveraging Qinchuan's long-term accumulation in thread grinding and heat treatment, the product can be adapted to humanoid robot lower limb joints, heavy-duty electric cylinders, and aerospace actuators.

The planetary roller screw engages multiple rollers simultaneously with the screw and nut threads, achieving higher load capacity and impact resistance than ball screws. Qinchuan Machine Tool is one of the few domestic companies capable of batch production of planetary roller screws and is actively promoting high-end import substitution.

## Selection and Deployment Recommendations

- During selection, clearly define load, speed, stroke, accuracy, and installation space. It is recommended to request samples for life and stiffness testing.
- Before deployment, pay attention to lubrication methods and dust-proof design to prevent foreign objects from entering the roller recirculation area and affecting lifespan.
