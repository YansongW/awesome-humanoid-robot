## Product Overview

SEM's wet lithium-ion battery separator is manufactured using a polyethylene (PE) biaxial stretching process, featuring high porosity, low thickness, excellent mechanical strength, and ionic conductivity. The product covers multiple thickness specifications from 7 µm to 25 µm and can be paired with ceramic/boehmite/PMMA coatings to further enhance thermal stability and safety.

The separator is one of the four main materials in lithium-ion batteries, directly determining battery internal resistance, safety performance, and cycle life. Humanoid robots and unmanned platforms have clear demands for high-safety, high-energy-density batteries, and SEM's separator is at the core of the upstream supply chain.

## Selection and Deployment Recommendations

- For high-energy-density batteries, it is recommended to use 7–9 µm thin base film with ceramic coating.
- For high-safety scenarios (e.g., robots), double-sided coated separators can be prioritized.
