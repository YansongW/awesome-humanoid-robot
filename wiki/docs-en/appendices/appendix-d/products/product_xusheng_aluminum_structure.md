## Product Overview

Xusheng Group's aluminum alloy structural parts are manufactured using high-pressure die casting, low-pressure casting, and precision CNC machining, targeting the lightweight needs of new energy vehicle three-electric systems, chassis systems, and humanoid robots. The products feature high specific strength, good thermal conductivity, and machinability, enabling integrated forming of complex geometric shapes.

The company has established a comprehensive precision manufacturing system centered around key customers such as Tesla. Its products cover motor housings, battery pack end plates, chassis control arms, and robot joint housings, making it an important participant in the lightweight supply chain for new energy vehicles and robots.

## Selection and Deployment Recommendations

- High-pressure die-cast part designs should avoid thick and large cross-sections, and rationally arrange gating and venting systems to reduce porosity.
- T6/T7 heat treatment is recommended for load-bearing structural parts to enhance strength and elongation.
- When assembling with magnesium alloy parts, pay attention to galvanic corrosion protection; use insulating gaskets or coating isolation.
