## Product Overview

Rollvis planetary roller screws are benchmark products in the global high-load linear transmission field, widely used in aerospace, machine tools, medical, injection molding, and humanoid robots. The R series adopts a planetary roller circulation structure, providing significantly higher load capacity and service life than ball screws in the same volume, making it an important imported option for high-load joints in humanoid robots.

Rollvis was acquired by Nidec-Shimpo in 2022, further strengthening its supply chain capabilities in the precision transmission field.

- **Humanoid Robots**: High-load linear joints such as hips, knees, and ankles.
- **Aerospace**: Aircraft control surfaces, landing gear, cabin door electric actuators.
- **Machine Tools**: Heavy cutting feed axes, large gantry feeds.
- **Injection Molding and Die Casting**: Clamping mechanisms, ejection mechanisms.

## Selection and Deployment Recommendations

- For high-load applications, clearly define dynamic load, rotational speed, acceleration, and service life requirements; it is recommended to contact the manufacturer for customized design.
- During installation, ensure coaxiality between the screw and the load; lubrication method and maintenance cycle directly affect service life.
