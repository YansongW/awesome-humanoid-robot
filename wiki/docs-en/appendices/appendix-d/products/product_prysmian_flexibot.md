## Product Overview

Prysmian Group Flexibot Robot Cable is a high-flexibility PUR robot cable launched by Prysmian for industrial robots, collaborative robots, and humanoid robot joints. With standardized interfaces, stable contact, and high-reliability industrial-grade design, this product meets the long-term stable requirements of robot systems for signal, power, or data connections.

## Selection and Deployment Recommendations

- Before selection, confirm the number of pins, current, voltage, protection level, and installation space. Request official datasheets and samples if necessary.
- For robot applications, focus on evaluating vibration resistance, bending resistance, oil resistance, and EMC shielding requirements. Prototype verification is recommended.
- For large-volume procurement, it is recommended to confirm delivery time, MOQ, and RoHS/REACH compliance certificates through authorized distributors or the original manufacturer.
