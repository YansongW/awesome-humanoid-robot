## Product Overview

Independent tooth profile and bearing design, high rigidity, long lifespan, suitable for heavy-duty industrial robots and high-torque joints of humanoid robots. This series is launched by Greatoo Intelligent Equipment, mainly targeting the RV reducer / tire mold / industrial robot / precision machinery market, with core parameters such as 30–200:1, applicable to robots, automation equipment, and precision transmission scenarios.

As one of the representative products of Greatoo Intelligent Equipment, the RV reducer is widely used in fields such as industrial robots and humanoid robots. The product adopts mature manufacturing processes and can provide customized interfaces, wiring methods, and control protocols according to customer requirements.
