## Product Overview

Automotive final assembly logistics handling, warehousing and distribution, elderly care and companionship, education and exhibition.

EFORT Yobot W1 is a representative product of EFORT Intelligent Equipment. It features a wheeled humanoid design, 32 degrees of freedom, and is based on EFORT's "Universal Technology Platform for Intelligent Robots." It has been validated for handling tasks in automotive final assembly logistics scenarios. Main specifications include: Not disclosed (dimensions), Not disclosed (weight), 32 DOF (degrees of freedom).
