## Product Overview

Brushless design, long life, low EMI, suitable for robot joints and actuators requiring high reliability and low maintenance. This series is launched by Johnson Electric, targeting the micro motor / actuator / drive module market, with core parameters such as 12–48 VDC, applicable to robotics, automotive electronics, and automation equipment scenarios.

As one of Johnson Electric's representative products, the ECI series brushless DC motors are widely used in automotive actuators, medical ventilators, small robot joints, and other fields. The products adopt mature manufacturing processes and can provide customized shaft extensions, connectors, and control protocols according to customer requirements.
