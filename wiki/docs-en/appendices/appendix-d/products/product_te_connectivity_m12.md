## Product Overview

The TE Connectivity M12 X-Code Industrial Ethernet Connector is an M12 X-Code circular connector launched by TE Connectivity for industrial robots, collaborative robots, sensor networks, and vision systems. This product meets the long-term stable requirements of robot systems for signal, power, or data connections through standardized interfaces, stable contact, and highly reliable industrial-grade design.
