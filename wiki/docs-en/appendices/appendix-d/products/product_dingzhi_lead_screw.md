## Product Overview

Integrated stepper/servo motor + lead screw, compact structure, good self-locking performance, suitable for robotic linear joints and precision push rods. This series of products is launched by Dingzhi Technology, mainly targeting the stepper motor / linear actuator / lead screw / joint module market, with core parameters such as 10–300 mm (customizable), applicable to robots, automation equipment, and precision transmission scenarios.

As one of the representative products of Dingzhi Technology, the ball screw / lead screw linear actuator is widely used in medical, 3D printing, robotics, and other fields. The product adopts mature manufacturing processes and can provide customized interfaces, wiring methods, and control protocols according to customer requirements.
