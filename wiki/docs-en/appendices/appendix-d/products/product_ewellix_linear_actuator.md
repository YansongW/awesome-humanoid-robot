## Product Overview

Ewellix electromechanical cylinders inherit SKF linear drive technology, offering a complete electromechanical actuation solution from compact low-thrust to industrial high-thrust applications. The CASM / LAMBDA series products feature modularity, high energy efficiency, and intelligent control, serving as replacements for traditional hydraulic and pneumatic cylinders.

After being acquired by Triton in 2023, Ewellix operates independently, continuing to serve the industrial automation, medical, robotics, and mobile machinery markets.

## Selection and Deployment Recommendations

- Choose the CASM or LAMBDA series based on thrust, speed, stroke, and accuracy requirements; pay attention to side load limits.
- For high-speed, long-stroke applications, verify the critical speed of the screw and configure a guide mechanism to share side loads.
