## Product Overview

The Agile Robots humanoid five-fingered intelligent dexterous hand originates from the technological accumulation of the German Aerospace Center (DLR). It adopts modular fingers and an active opposing thumb, integrates multi-sensor fusion grasping algorithms, and features highly biomimetic motion trajectories and compliant interaction capabilities.
