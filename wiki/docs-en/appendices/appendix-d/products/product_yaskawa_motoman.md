## Product Overview

The MOTOMAN HC20DTP is a high-payload collaborative robot launched by Yaskawa Electric Corporation, supporting a 20 kg payload and IP67 wrist protection, enabling human-robot collaboration in high-sanitation standards and harsh environments.
