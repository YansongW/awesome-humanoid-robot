## Product Overview

Kaidi linear drives are electromechanical products that use permanent magnet DC/brushless motors as the core, combined with lead screw or gear transmission to achieve linear motion. The products feature low noise, modularity, and easy integration, and are widely used in smart homes, medical rehabilitation, automotive seats, and humanoid robot linear joints.

Leveraging its large-scale manufacturing and cost control capabilities, Kaidi has become one of the major export enterprises in the domestic linear drive field and is actively upgrading toward high thrust density and intelligent robot actuators.

## Selection and Deployment Recommendations

- When selecting a model, match the motor specifications based on load, speed, stroke, and installation space, and note the speed difference between no-load and full-load conditions.
- For medical or outdoor applications, confirm the protection rating, EMC certification, and noise requirements, and choose models with Hall feedback if necessary.
