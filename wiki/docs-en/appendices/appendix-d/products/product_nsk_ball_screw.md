## Product Overview

NSK ball screws are high-precision transmission products launched by NSK for the machine tool, semiconductor, medical, and robotics sectors. High-end models such as the NSK S1 series are renowned for high speed, low noise, long life, and excellent dust-proofing/low particle generation performance, making them a mainstream choice for imported components in high-end equipment.

Leveraging its deep expertise in bearings, materials, and lubrication technology, NSK provides complete solutions ranging from standard ball screws to products for special environments.

## Selection and Deployment Recommendations

- For high-speed applications, pay attention to DN value limits and select appropriate lead and preload to avoid temperature rise and noise issues.
- For cleanroom or vacuum environments, choose NSK low particle generation/vacuum compatible specifications and use dedicated grease.
