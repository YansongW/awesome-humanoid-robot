## Product Overview

The JST PH Series 2.0 mm Wire-to-Board Connector is a 2.0 mm wire-to-board connector launched by Japan Solderless Terminal for small motors, sensors, controllers, and drones. This product features a standardized interface, stable contact, and high-reliability industrial-grade design, meeting the long-term stable requirements of robot systems for signal, power, or data connections.
