## Product Overview

The JAE IL-WX 1.25 mm Wire-to-Board Connector is a 1.25 mm wire-to-board connector launched by Japan Aviation Electronics for industrial controllers, internal robot wiring, and camera modules. This product features a standardized interface, stable contact, and high-reliability industrial-grade design, meeting the long-term stability requirements of robot systems for signal, power, or data connections.
