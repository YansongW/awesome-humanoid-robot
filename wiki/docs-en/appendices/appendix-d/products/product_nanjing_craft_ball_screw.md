## Product Overview

The ball screw pair of Nanjing Craft Equipment Manufacturing Co., Ltd. is one of the representative products of domestic rolling functional components, covering a full range of specifications from micro to large. The products are widely used in CNC machine tools, robots, semiconductor equipment, and automated production lines, serving as an important option for domestic substitution of high-end screws.

Nanjing Craft possesses complete manufacturing capabilities from material heat treatment and precision grinding to dynamic testing, and can provide ground-grade P1 and above precision products to meet high-precision positioning and repeat positioning requirements.

## Selection and Deployment Recommendations

- Select the corresponding grade from P1 to P7 based on the precision requirements of the machine tool or robot, and pay attention to the impact of preload on stiffness.
- For long-stroke applications, it is recommended to verify the critical speed and column stability of the screw, and add supports or choose a larger diameter if necessary.
