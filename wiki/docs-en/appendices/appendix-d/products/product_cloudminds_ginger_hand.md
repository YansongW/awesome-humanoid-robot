## Product Overview

The Ginger 2 Dexterous Hand is an end effector developed by CloudMinds for the Cloud Ginger 2 cloud-based intelligent service robot. Leveraging the CloudMinds HaiRui Cloud Brain and the RobotGPT multimodal large model, it achieves high-precision flexible grasping and human-robot interaction guided by vision.
