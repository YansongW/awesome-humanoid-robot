## Product Overview

CSB self-lubricating bearings use a metal-polymer composite material, with bronze powder and a PTFE lubricating layer sintered onto a steel backing, achieving maintenance-free, low-friction, and wear-resistant sliding support. This product series covers various forms such as bushings, liners, thrust washers, and spherical plain bearings, and can replace traditional grease-lubricated bearings, especially suitable for applications where frequent maintenance is difficult or in contaminated environments.

In humanoid robot applications, self-lubricating bearings can be used in joint swing parts, linkage hinge points, and linear actuator guide sleeves, reducing maintenance requirements and improving overall machine reliability.

## Selection and Deployment Recommendations

- When selecting, calculate the actual load, speed, temperature, and environmental medium to ensure the PV value is within the allowable range.
- During press-fitting, use a mandrel or dedicated tooling to avoid deformation of the bearing bore affecting the fit.
- In chemically corrosive or underwater conditions, choose fiber-wound or stainless steel backing models.
