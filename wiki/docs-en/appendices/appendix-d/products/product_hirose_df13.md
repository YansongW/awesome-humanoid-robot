## Product Overview

The Hirose DF13 1.25 mm Wire-to-Board Connector is a 1.25 mm wire-to-board connector launched by Hirose Electric for small servos, encoders, industrial cameras, and drones. This product features a standardized interface, stable contact, and high-reliability industrial-grade design, meeting the long-term stable requirements of robot systems for signal, power, or data connections.
