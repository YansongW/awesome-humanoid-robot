## Product Overview

The external rotor motor features a compact structure, high inertia, and smooth operation, making it suitable for direct-drive fans and space-constrained thermal management applications. This product series is launched by Weiguang and primarily targets the motor/fan/drive control market, with core parameters such as 5–200 W, and is applicable to cold chain, communication, and robot thermal management scenarios.

As one of Weiguang's representative products, the external rotor motor is widely used in cold chain equipment fans, robot thermal management, communication equipment heat dissipation, and other fields. The product adopts mature manufacturing processes and can provide customized voltage, speed, and installation methods according to customer requirements.
