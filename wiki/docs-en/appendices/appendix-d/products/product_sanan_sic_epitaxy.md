## Product Overview

Sanan Optoelectronics' 6-inch SiC epitaxy wafer uses chemical vapor deposition (CVD) homoepitaxial technology to grow a low-defect, high-uniformity N-type epitaxial layer on a 4H-SiC conductive substrate. It is a key intermediate material for manufacturing power devices such as SiC MOSFETs and SiC SBDs.

Leveraging its compound semiconductor platform, the company has deployed a full industrial chain covering SiC substrates, epitaxy, devices, and packaging, providing SiC power solutions for new energy vehicles, photovoltaic inverters, charging piles, and high-power motor controllers for robotics.

- For high-reliability applications, attention should be paid to epitaxial defect density and batch consistency.
