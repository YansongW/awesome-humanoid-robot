## Product Overview

The Elfin 5 is a 6-degree-of-freedom collaborative robot launched by Han's Robot, with a payload of 5 kg and a reach of 950 mm, designed for industrial flexible manufacturing and commercial services.

The product adopts a dual-joint modular design, inheriting Han's Laser's experience in precision manufacturing, emphasizing ease of use, safety, and an open ecosystem, supporting drag teaching and graphical programming.
