## Product Overview

The Amphenol Minitek MicroSpace 1.27 mm Wire-to-Board Connector is a 1.27 mm wire-to-board connector launched by Amphenol for automotive electronics, robot controllers, and sensor interfaces. This product features a standardized interface, stable contact, and high-reliability industrial-grade design to meet the long-term stable requirements of robot systems for signal, power, or data connections.
