## Product Overview

Taide deep groove ball bearings have a simple structure and are easy to use. They can withstand both radial and certain axial loads, making them one of the most widely used types of rolling bearings. The products are made of bearing steel GCr15, processed through heat treatment, precision grinding, and superfinishing. Accuracy grades cover P6 to P4, and various sealing forms are available, including open type, ZZ dust cover, and 2RS rubber seal.

The company has long supplied bearings for automotive air conditioning compressors, motors, and industrial equipment. In recent years, it has developed small-sized, high-speed, low-noise deep groove ball bearings to meet the demands of robot micro-joints, servo motors, and harmonic reducers.

## Selection and Deployment Recommendations

- Select the clearance group based on load direction and magnitude. For robot joints, C2 or CN group is recommended.
- For high-speed applications, ZZ dust cover or open structure is recommended, along with low-viscosity grease.
- When there is a risk of dust or liquid ingress, priority should be given to 2RS contact rubber seals.
