## Product Overview

Wanfeng Auto Wheels' magnesium alloy die-cast structural parts leverage the global die-casting technology accumulation of its subsidiary Meridian, using high-pressure die-casting processes to produce complex thin-walled structural parts. The product range covers automotive instrument panel brackets, seat frames, front-end modules, and lightweight structural parts for robots, offering advantages such as significant weight reduction, high integration, and damping vibration reduction.

The company possesses a cluster of 350–4,000 ton die-casting equipment, with integrated capabilities in mold design, die-casting molding, CNC machining, and surface treatment, providing large-scale integrated die-casting solutions for new energy vehicles and humanoid robots.

## Selection and Deployment Recommendations

- Avoid sharp corners and abrupt wall thickness changes during design; set appropriate draft angles and gating systems.
- Key load-bearing areas should be verified through FE analysis, and machining allowances should be reserved.
- Use anti-corrosion gaskets or coatings during assembly to prevent galvanic corrosion from direct contact between magnesium alloy and dissimilar metals.
