## Product Overview

SKF Linear Motion System integrates bearing, sealing, lubrication, and linear guide/screw technologies to provide long-life, low-maintenance linear motion solutions for industrial automation, machine tools, medical equipment, and robotics. The product line covers ball guides, roller guides, ball screws, electric cylinders, and engineering services.

Part of SKF's linear motion business has been spun off as Ewellix, but the SKF brand remains in the fields of bearings, seals, and rotating equipment solutions, continuing to influence the linear motion market through technology licensing and collaboration.

## Selection and Deployment Recommendations

- When selecting, consider SKF's lubrication and sealing solutions to leverage their long-life and low-maintenance advantages.
- In heavy-load or contaminated environments, roller guides with protective covers and centralized lubrication systems are recommended.
