## Product Overview

XPeng Motors' Guangzhou factory P7 production training, screw tightening, material sorting, production form inspection; future applications include store tours and commercial services.

Iron (1st generation) is the flagship product of XPeng Robotics. It features the self-developed Turing AI chip (40 cores, 3000 T computing power), 720° eagle-eye vision, Tianji AIOS, end-to-end large model + reinforcement learning. Key specifications include: 178 cm (height), 70 kg (weight), 62 active degrees of freedom (DOF).
