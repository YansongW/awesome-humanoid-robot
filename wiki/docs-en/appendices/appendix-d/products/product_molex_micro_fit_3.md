## Product Overview

The Molex Micro-Fit 3.0 Connector System is a 3.00 mm wire-to-board/wire-to-wire connector launched by Molex for robot power distribution, servo drives, and controller I/O. This product meets the long-term stability requirements of robot systems for signal, power, or data connections through standardized interfaces, stable contact, and high-reliability industrial-grade design.
