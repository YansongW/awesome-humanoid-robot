## Product Overview

The JONHON circular connector series inherits aviation-grade high-reliability design concepts, providing multi-core, multi-shell, and multi-locking form electrical signal and power interconnection solutions. The products are widely used in defense, communications, new energy vehicles, and industrial equipment. In recent years, customized models have been launched for the high-vibration, high-bending, and high-protection requirements of robots.

The connector shell is made of high-strength aluminum alloy or stainless steel, and the contacts are made of copper alloy with gold/nickel plating. It features vibration resistance, salt spray resistance, and electromagnetic interference protection, meeting the demanding working conditions of humanoid robot joints and end effectors.

## Selection and Deployment Recommendations

- When selecting a model, clearly determine the number of cores, current, voltage, protection rating, and locking method to avoid incorrect insertion on site.
- For high-voltage power circuits, it is recommended to choose models with shielding and interlock structures, and perform insulation and dielectric withstanding voltage tests.
- For robot joint applications, it is recommended to prioritize models with anti-bending cable accessories to extend service life.
