## Product Overview

Hengli Hydraulic Cylinders are high-end hydraulic actuation components widely adopted by global construction machinery OEMs, covering excavators, tunnel boring machines, marine applications, wind power installation, and metallurgical equipment. The products are known for high pressure, long service life, and high reliability. Meanwhile, leveraging its precision hydraulic manufacturing capabilities, the company has expanded into the humanoid robot market by developing roller screws and electric cylinders.

Hydraulic cylinders achieve high-thrust linear motion through liquid pressure, complementing electric cylinders, which are suited for high power density and high-precision control scenarios, respectively.

## Selection and Deployment Recommendations

- Selection requires clarification of working pressure, bore diameter, stroke, mounting method, and environmental medium, matching seal grade and coating.
- High-pressure, long-stroke cylinders require stability verification; regularly inspect seals and hydraulic oil cleanliness to extend service life.
