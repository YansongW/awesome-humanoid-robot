## Product Overview

Slotless coreless winding, low inertia, high dynamic response, suitable for driving in confined spaces such as robot fingers and dexterous hands. This series is launched by MOONS' Electric, primarily targeting the motor/drive/motion control market, with core parameters such as 12–48 VDC, applicable to robotics, automation equipment, and precision transmission scenarios.

As one of MOONS' Electric's representative products, the coreless brushless DC motor is widely used in 3C, semiconductor, medical equipment, and other fields. The product adopts mature manufacturing processes and can provide customized interfaces, wiring methods, and control protocols according to customer requirements.
