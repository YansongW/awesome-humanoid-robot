## Product Overview

The HIWIN FSI series is a rolled-grade ball screw, featuring a double-cut flange single nut and zero-backlash preload options. Taking the R16-5T3 as an example, the screw diameter is 16 mm, lead is 5 mm, and the rolled accuracy grade is C7, suitable for general automation and cost-sensitive linear positioning applications.

The FSI series can reach a maximum length of approximately 3,000 mm, with a Dm-N value of up to about 220,000. It can be paired with HIWIN HG/EG linear guides and servo motors to form KK linear modules or humanoid robot linear joints. Ground grades C5/C3 are available for higher precision requirements.

## Selection and Deployment Recommendations

For general positioning, the C7 rolled grade is sufficient; for high precision or long-life applications, upgrading to C5/C3 ground grade with preloaded nuts is recommended.
