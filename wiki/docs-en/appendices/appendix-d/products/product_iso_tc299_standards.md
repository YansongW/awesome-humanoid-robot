## Product Overview

The ISO/TC 299 Robotics Standards family is a global collection of robotics technical specifications led by the International Organization for Standardization, covering safety requirements, test methods, terminology definitions, and interoperability frameworks for industrial, service, collaborative, and humanoid robots. This standards family provides internationally recognized compliance benchmarks for robot design, manufacturing, integration, and operation, and has been adopted as national standards or referenced in regulations by major markets such as the European Union, the United States, and China.

## Application Scenarios

- **Industrial Robot Safety**: ISO 10218 provides safety design and integration requirements for robots and robot systems.
- **Collaborative Robots**: ISO/TS 15066 defines safety requirements, force/torque limits, and verification methods for collaborative operations.
- **Personal Care Robots**: ISO 13482 addresses safety requirements for mobile service robots and physical assistant robots.
- **Humanoid Robot Compliance**: Serves as the underlying standard reference for safety assessment of complete machines and system integration.
