## Product Overview

Lixing Precision Bearing Steel Balls are core rolling elements for bearings, ball screws, and precision transmission devices. The product is made of high-carbon chromium bearing steel GCr15, manufactured through multiple processes including cold heading, ball grinding, heat treatment, hard grinding, fine grinding, and super finishing. Dimensional accuracy can reach G5 grade, with low ball batch diameter variation, low surface roughness, and high fatigue life.

As the component that directly bears contact stress in bearings, the quality of steel balls directly determines bearing noise, vibration, life, and reliability. Lixing products are used by international and domestic mainstream bearing manufacturers and are expanding into high-precision steel balls for robot joint bearings, ball screws, and harmonic reducers.

## Selection and Deployment Recommendations

- For high-precision bearings, select G10 grade or above steel balls; for general industrial bearings, G16–G40 is acceptable.
- Steel ball material and hardness should match the ring, cage, and lubrication method to avoid electrical corrosion or fretting wear.
- During storage and transportation, prevent collision, rust, and contamination; use dedicated containers.
