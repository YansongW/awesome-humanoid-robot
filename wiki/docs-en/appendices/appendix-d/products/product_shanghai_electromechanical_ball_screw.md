## Product Overview

High-precision grinding process, low friction, high rigidity, enabling precise linear positioning and repetitive motion. This series is launched by Shanghai Electromechanical, primarily targeting the ball screw / elevator component / precision transmission market, with core parameters such as 8–80 mm, suitable for machine tools, robots, and automation equipment scenarios.

As one of the representative products of Shanghai Electromechanical, the ball screw is widely used in humanoid robot linear joints, machine tool feed axes, semiconductor equipment, and other fields. The product adopts mature manufacturing processes and can provide customized diameters, leads, accuracy grades, and preload methods according to customer requirements.
