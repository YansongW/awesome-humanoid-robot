## Product Overview

Helical planetary structure, low backlash, high rigidity, suitable for high-dynamic, high-precision positioning robot joints and servo systems. This series is launched by Nidec-Shimpo, primarily targeting the reducer / transmission component / motor module market, with core parameters such as 3:1 – 100:1, applicable to industrial robots, collaborative robots, and humanoid robot scenarios.

As one of Nidec-Shimpo's representative products, the VRSF series planetary gearbox is widely used in industrial robot joints, semiconductor equipment, CNC machine tools, and other fields. The product adopts mature manufacturing processes and can provide customized flanges, output shafts, lubrication, and sealing configurations according to customer requirements.
