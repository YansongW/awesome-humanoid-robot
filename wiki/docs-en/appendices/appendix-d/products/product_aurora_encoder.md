## Product Overview

The JZN/JKN series optical angle encoders developed by Yuheng Optics, a subsidiary of Aurora Optics, are representative products for domestic substitution of high-end encoders. This series adopts metal grating technology, featuring high precision, high reliability, and good environmental adaptability, with performance indicators reaching the advanced level of similar international products.

The company has publicly disclosed that its highest-precision absolute optical encoder can reach 29 bits, and the products have passed multiple authoritative certifications including EU RoHS, CE, explosion-proof, and military product experimental verification. The JZN/JKN series is mainly targeted at application scenarios requiring extremely high position feedback accuracy, such as high-end CNC machine tools, servo motors, robot joints, aerospace, and military measurement and control.

## Application Scenarios

- **High-end CNC Machine Tools**: Precision angle feedback for rotary tables, tool magazines, and fourth/fifth axes.
- **Servo Motors**: Rotary position and speed feedback to improve control accuracy.
- **Humanoid Robot Joints**: High-precision joint angle measurement to support motion control.
- **Aerospace/Military**: Radar antenna mounts, photoelectric theodolites, precision rotary tables.

## Selection and Deployment Recommendations

- For high-end applications, confirm the compatibility of encoder resolution, accuracy, interface protocol with the CNC system/servo drive.
- During installation, strictly control shaft concentricity and face runout to ensure the actual accuracy of the optical encoder.
