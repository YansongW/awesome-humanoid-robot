## Product Overview

High power density, low cogging torque, support for multiple feedback interfaces, suitable for robot joints and servo systems requiring high dynamic response. This series is launched by Nidec Corporation, targeting the motor / reducer / drive module market, with core parameters such as 50 W–5 kW, applicable to robots, automation equipment, and precision transmission scenarios.

As one of Nidec's representative products, the NX Series Servo Motor is widely used in industrial robots, humanoid robot joints, semiconductor equipment, and other fields. The product adopts mature manufacturing processes and can provide customized flange, shaft extension, encoder, and brake configurations according to customer requirements.
