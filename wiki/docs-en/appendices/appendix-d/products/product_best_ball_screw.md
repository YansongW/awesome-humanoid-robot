## Product Overview

High-precision grinding, low friction, high transmission efficiency, customizable planetary roller screws to meet high-load robot joints. This series is launched by Best Precision Machinery, targeting the precision components / ball screws / linear guides / automotive parts market, with core parameters such as C3–C10, suitable for robots, automation equipment, and precision transmission scenarios.

As one of the representative products of Best Precision Machinery, ball screws are widely used in fields such as fuel vehicles and hybrids. The product adopts mature manufacturing processes and can provide customized interfaces, wiring methods, and control protocols according to customer requirements.
