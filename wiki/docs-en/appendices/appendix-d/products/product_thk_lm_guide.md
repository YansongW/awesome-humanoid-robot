## Product Overview

THK LM Guide is the world's first linear motion guide, pioneering rolling linear motion technology. Series such as SHS, SSR, SNR/SNS are renowned for low friction, high rigidity, and high positioning accuracy, serving as core guiding components for machine tools, semiconductor equipment, robots, and automation platforms.

THK continuously releases versions with dust protection, low particle generation, corrosion resistance, and intelligent monitoring to meet diverse needs ranging from cleanrooms to heavy cutting.

## Selection and Deployment Recommendations

- Select block type and quantity based on load direction and magnitude. For high-precision applications, recommend grade P or above and use in pairs.
- The mounting surface for the rail must ensure flatness and parallelism. Grease replenishment intervals depend on the environment and operating speed.
