## Product Overview

TÜV SÜD Robot Safety Certification Service is a third-party compliance solution for industrial robots, collaborative robots, service robots, and humanoid robots. The service covers safety design review, risk assessment, type testing, functional safety assessment, electromagnetic compatibility testing, and CE/UKCA marking support, helping manufacturers meet the requirements of the EU Machinery Directive, Low Voltage Directive, EMC Directive, and regulations in major global markets.
