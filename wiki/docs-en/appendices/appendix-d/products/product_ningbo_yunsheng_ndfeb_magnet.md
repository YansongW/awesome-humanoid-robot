## Product Overview

Ningbo Yunsheng's high-performance sintered NdFeB magnets adopt the Nd₂Fe₁₄B-based sintered magnet technology route, covering common grades such as N, M, H, SH, and UH. They can be customized into complex magnetic circuit shapes such as arc segments, blocks, and rings. The products feature high remanence, high maximum energy product, and high coercivity, and are widely used in new energy vehicle drive motors, industrial servo motors, wind turbines, and humanoid robot joint actuators.

Leveraging a dual-core business layout of magnetic materials and servo motors, Ningbo Yunsheng possesses collaborative development capabilities from magnets to motor actuators, providing integrated support for materials and drive solutions to robotics customers.

## Selection and Deployment Recommendations

- For high-dynamic joint motors, it is recommended to select SH/UH grades or higher and verify the high-temperature demagnetization curve.
- The surface protection of the magnet must match the humidity and corrosion risks of the motor's operating environment.
