### 公司简介

拓普集团是中国领先的汽车 NVH、底盘与轻量化结构件供应商，正将精密机加工、压铸与机电集成能力延伸至机器人结构件与执行器领域。

公司主营减震器、内饰、底盘系统、热管理、汽车电子与轻量化车身等产品，客户覆盖国内外主流新能源车企。面向人形机器人浪潮，拓普依托大型压铸、机加工与总成能力，布局机器人机电执行器、关节模组壳体与轻量化结构件，目标是成为机器人核心结构件与执行器总成供应商。

### Company Profile

Tuopu Group is a leading Chinese supplier of automotive NVH, chassis, and lightweight structural parts. It is extending its precision machining, die-casting, and electromechanical integration capabilities into the field of robot structural parts and actuators.

The company's main products include shock absorbers, interiors, chassis systems, thermal management, automotive electronics, and lightweight body parts, serving both domestic and international mainstream new energy vehicle manufacturers. In response to the wave of humanoid robots, Tuopu leverages its large-scale die-casting, machining, and assembly capabilities to develop robot electromechanical actuators, joint module housings, and lightweight structural parts, aiming to become a core supplier of robot structural parts and actuator assemblies.
