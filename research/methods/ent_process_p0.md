## Overview

Project initiation and business baseline mark the starting point of humanoid robot product development, covering market needs analysis, technical feasibility assessment, business model definition, initial budgeting and milestone planning, and core team formation. This phase delivers a business plan, preliminary product specifications, and a project charter to inform subsequent design, R&D, and mass production.
