The framework also incorporates a generative module that can automatically generate scene layouts, object properties, and task trajectories based on task descriptions or environmental constraints, significantly reducing manual configuration costs. (c) Key Technological Innovations: The main innovations of Genesis are reflected in three aspects. Finally, Genesis makes a breakthrough in physical accuracy by introducing high-order approximations of contact dynamics and adaptive time-stepping algorithms, which, while maintaining real-time performance, significantly enhance the consistency between simulation results and the real world, providing more reliable support for sim-to-real transfer.
