**研究背景与问题**：在家庭服务、医疗辅助及协作制造等应用场景中，人形机器人需要频繁地与人类或物体发生物理接触，例如握手、递物或搬运。这类接触丰富的任务要求机器人不仅具备精准的末端执行器控制能力，还必须展现出高度的顺应性，以避免对人或物体造成损伤。然而，传统的运动规划与控制方法往往将任务分解为独立的感知、规划与控制模块，各模块间的接口误差和延迟会累积，导致整体性能下降。此外，基于模型的控制方法难以处理非结构化环境中的不确定性和动态变化。因此，如何构建一个能够从视觉输入直接映射到柔顺动作的统一控制框架，成为该领域亟待解决的关键问题。

**方法或模型框架**：GentleHumanoid 提出了一种分层但紧密耦合的控制架构。首先，系统从多视角相机图像、本体状态（如关节角度与力矩）以及人类演示视频或动捕轨迹中，通过视觉感知模块恢复场景几何、目标物体位姿及运动表征。随后，这些高层语义信息被输入至一个基于近端策略优化（PPO）的强化学习策略网络，该网络负责生成末端执行器（腕手）的期望目标位姿与力/力矩指令。最关键的设计在于，该强化学习策略并非独立运行，而是与一个全身控制器（WBC/MPC）在同一训练与部署链路中协同工作。全身控制器接收策略输出的高层目标，并实时求解最优的全身关节力矩，同时保证运动学与动力学约束的满足。

## Overview

When humanoid robots perform tasks involving close interaction with humans and objects, achieving safe, compliant, and efficient upper-body motion control is one of the core challenges in robotics. Existing methods typically separate high-level task planning from low-level motion control, leading to significant discontinuities and gaps in the transition from abstract goals to concrete actions. GentleHumanoid proposes a novel end-to-end training and deployment framework that integrates reinforcement learning (PPO/RL) policies with whole-body controllers (WBC/MPC) within a unified pipeline, enabling effective learning of upper-body compliance in contact-rich scenarios, thereby enhancing robot robustness and safety in complex interaction tasks.

## Content

**Research Background and Problem**: In application scenarios such as home service, medical assistance, and collaborative manufacturing, humanoid robots frequently engage in physical contact with humans or objects, e.g., handshakes, object handovers, or carrying tasks. These contact-rich tasks require robots not only to possess precise end-effector control but also to exhibit high compliance to avoid harming humans or objects. However, traditional motion planning and control methods often decompose tasks into independent modules for perception, planning, and control, where interface errors and delays between modules accumulate, degrading overall performance. Moreover, model-based control methods struggle to handle uncertainties and dynamic changes in unstructured environments. Therefore, constructing a unified control framework that directly maps visual input to compliant actions has become a critical issue in this field.

**Method or Model Framework**: GentleHumanoid proposes a hierarchical yet tightly coupled control architecture. First, the system recovers scene geometry, target object poses, and motion representations from multi-view camera images, proprioceptive states (e.g., joint angles and torques), and human demonstration videos or motion capture trajectories via a visual perception module. These high-level semantic inputs are then fed into a reinforcement learning policy network based on Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO), which generates desired target poses and force/torque commands for the end-effector (wrist/hand). The key design is that this RL policy does not operate independently but collaborates with a whole-body controller (WBC/MPC) within the same training and deployment pipeline. The whole-body controller receives the high-level targets output by the policy and solves for optimal whole-body joint torques in real time while satisfying kinematic and dynamic constraints.

**Key Technical Innovations**: The core innovation of GentleHumanoid lies in breaking the traditional barrier between high-level policies and low-level controllers. In previous research, RL policies typically output joint angles or velocities as references for low-level controllers, which process these references independently, resulting in information loss and response delays. GentleHumanoid places the PPO/RL policy and WBC/MPC within the same training pipeline, allowing the policy to directly perceive the dynamic response characteristics of the whole-body controller during training, thereby learning high-level commands that are more physically compliant and constraint-aware. Additionally, by introducing a visual closed-loop mechanism, the policy can dynamically adjust contact forces and motion trajectories based on real-time visual feedback, significantly improving adaptability in dynamic environments. This end-to-end joint training paradigm effectively reduces discontinuities between high-level task goals and low-level joint actions, enabling smoother and safer interaction behaviors.

**Experiments/Validation and Application Value**: Although the paper does not disclose specific quantitative experimental results, it can be reasonably inferred from the methodological design that GentleHumanoid offers significant advantages in various contact-rich tasks. For example, in scenarios where a robot receives an object from a human hand or assists in upper-limb rehabilitation training, the framework can automatically adjust grasping forces and motion speeds through learned compliant strategies, avoiding sudden rigid impacts. Its application value extends beyond home service robots to fields such as medical rehabilitation, flexible assembly lines, and human-robot collaboration workstations, where safety is paramount. By combining the flexibility of reinforcement learning with the precision of model predictive control, GentleHumanoid provides a feasible technical pathway for humanoid robots to achieve safe and natural physical interaction in unstructured environments.
