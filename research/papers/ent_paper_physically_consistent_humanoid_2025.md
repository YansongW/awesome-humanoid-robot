Additionally, the mapping from visual input to motion output often suffers from physical inconsistencies, such as generated actions violating joint limits or ground contact conditions. This issue is particularly pronounced in tasks requiring simultaneous scene understanding, object interaction, and whole-body coordination, becoming a key bottleneck limiting the practical deployment of humanoid robots.

This design naturally handles the multimodality of actions, such as generating diverse reasonable motion strategies under different terrains or object positions.
